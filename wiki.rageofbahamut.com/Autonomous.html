Doublefate Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Up to four Legends guaranteed!

Draw enough packs and you'll get to choose between two Legend cards.

Keep drawing for up to four copies!

Light Box Bonuses[edit]

Draws SR-L Drop Rates Ultimate Powders
1 Standart 1
2 3 Times 2
3 5 Times 3
4 7 Times 4
5+ 10 Times 5

Ultra Box Bonuses[edit]

Draws SR-L Drop Rates Ultimate Powders
1 Standart 10
2 5 Times 15
3 10 Times 20
4 15 Times 25
5+ 20 Times 30

Doublefate Card Pack Details[edit]

  • This pack can be purchased at the Lite price point or the Ultra price point. Every draw includes a card from the Light box or the Ultra box.
  • The Lite box can be refilled after a designated S Rare - Legend card is drawn.
  • The Ultra box can be refilled after a Legend card is drawn. (On the Card Pack screen, you can select which of two Legends will appear.)
  • Each of the boxes can be refilled three times. That means that if you buy enough packs, four copies of a great card are sure to drop. Once the last Lite and Ultra boxes have been emptied, the card pack ends.
  • For your second through fifth packs on either price point, drop rates rise and more ultimate powders are included. These return to their default values once the box gets reset, but rise again after subsequent purchases.

Doublefate Claim Tickets[edit]

  • Doublefate Ultra claim tickets and Doublefate Lite claim tickets can be obtained in events.
  • These tickets let you draw a single card from on of the two boxes.
  • Doublefate claim tickets don't expire. They can be carried over for the next Doublefate card pack.
  • Details are subject to change.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
