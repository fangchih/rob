Story[edit]

A city of gold once shone upon the world as a beacon of prosperity. That shimmering oasis now stands half-ruined by leagues of undead soldiers.

When you hear of the city's plight, you rush to its gates. There you encounter a mysterious figure who beckons you inside and leads you to a beautiful statue of a queen. It is solid gold, save for a jeweled neck band....

What befell the city of abundance? Could there be more to this statue than meets the eye?

The answers are slumbering somewhere in this golden necropolis.

How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade! Silence the golden dead and compete to collect the most venom arrows! Collect minds and exchange them for great rewards!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more venom arrows than other brigades
  • Venom arrows drop when you silence the golden dead.
  • This event will have nine daily 19-hour campaigns. The number of venom arrows that brigades collect is calculated per campaign.
  • You must join a team to participate.
  • If you don't create or join a team by 10am Dec. 5th, 2013 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

Daily Play Bonus[edit]

Gold balms and average minds will be available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting from 5 AM (ET).

  • One gold balm recovers 30 stamina during the event
  • Average minds can be exchanged for rewards. On the Golden Necropolis screen, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Minds button.

Free Card[edit]

Visit the main Golden Necropolis screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available in the quest; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 Lone Flames. In battles against the golden dead, lone flames perfectly silence all enemies. In battles against treasure hunters, lone flames boost ATK by 20%

Lone flames will change into holy powder (personal) after the Golden Necropolis event ends.
