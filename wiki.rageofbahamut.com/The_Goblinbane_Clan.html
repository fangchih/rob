Story[edit]

Vengeful mouse royalty? Check.

Gruff bodyguard? Check.

Horde of angry goblins? You know it.


Princess Daffodil and Bolt are all ready to lay down revenge

on the kingdom-thieving Goblin Emperor. Let's hope

you can keep up on the journey down into the Crystal Caves...


How to Play[edit]

Defeat bosses to get items! Use Items to train the Shogun!

Some items enhance ATK, others DEF!

The more you use, the better she gets!

Cards awarded after the event!


Gather echoes to make echo seals

Your order will get a big bonus to ranking shards!

Battle lots of raid bosses to up the bonus!


New Features[edit]

The Shogun
Train the Shogun using the tomes and spheres that raid bosses drop when defeated.
Users who train the shogun a lot will get a special Legend card at the end of the event. Users who didn't will still get a significantly weaker SS Rare card, but it won't be as good. The shogun card's exact stats, alignment, and evolution depend on the type and number of items used in training.
Daily Event Animations Replaced
Dialogue that was previously shown in daily animations now appears on the login bonus screen instead.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours.

  • One Elixir recovers 1 AP during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the The Goblinbane Clan page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.
