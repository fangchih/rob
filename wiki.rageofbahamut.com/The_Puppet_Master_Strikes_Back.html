About this event[edit]

Event Details[edit]

This Golem Master terrorizing our town used to be a good friend of mine. I am the Master Alchemist, and I will guide you to him. You won't let his giant golems destroy my town, will you?! Please protect us from the Golem Master!

About 'The Puppet Master'[edit]

  • Brave the event quests during the event and the Golem Master will appear!
  • Find and defeat him and his monsters to get Treasure Chests! Defeat them a certain number of times and you'll get special rewards!!!
  • Get 2x Damage Points when you attack Clay Golems! They only appear once in a while, so seek them out and rack up loads of Damage Points when you find them

Story[edit]

There was once a pure-hearted boy known throughout his town as a genius. Unfortunately the other townspeople, fearing his talents, bullied him and made him into an outcast.... Possessing the uncanny ability to create life out of nothing, the boy set out on a long journey to practice his trade as a puppeteer.... Many years passed, and the town almost forgot about him.... One day, the former puppet master returned from his long journey.... without a single remnant of the tenderhearted boy he once was. Hideous golems are now ravaging the town--the instruments of this rage-filled Golem Master's revenge.... Can you stop them??


Notes[edit]

About 'The Puppet Master'[edit]

  • Embark on the exclusive event quests and monsters will appear at a certain rate.
  • Monsters will not appear during regular quests.
  • There are multiple types of monsters, which may appear one after another after you defeat them.
  • You can still go on either event quests or regular quests while a monster is around.
  • Additional monsters will not appear while a monster is already present nor while you are cooperating with another user to fight their monster.

Monsters in this Event[edit]

  • Golem Master
    • The Golem Master will start appearing from LVL 1; defeat him and the next LVL will appear.
    • Defeat Golem Master LVL 15 and Golem Master+ will start appearing.
    • An even stronger Golem Master will appear once your Total Individual Damage reaches 1,000,000 and then at 2,000,000.
  • Clay Golem
    • These appear at a certain rate after you defeat monsters.
    • When you attack them, you will be able to give them twice the normal amount of damage.
  • Wight King (Bonus Monster)
    • He may appear after you defeat a monster after having defeated the highest LVL Golem Master.
    • When you attack him, you will be able to give him twice the normal amount of damage.
    • If you defeat him, the user who discovered him and cooperators who dealt at least 1,000,000 damage will receive a special reward.
    • Only the card Zhuque has an Event Skill that activates against the Wight King: 'Big boost to damage against Wight King in The Puppet Master.'
    • If you battle the Wight King using a deck filled with cards with other Event Skills, they will not activate at all.

Rewards for Defeating Monsters[edit]

  • You will be rewarded with Treasure Chests after defeating monsters.
  • You cannot get Treasure Chests for monsters unless you've dealt them damage.
  • Special Rewards are given to each player every time a certain number of monsters are defeated (see 'Rewards').
  • Obtained items and cards are sent to 'Presents.'

Order Rewards for Defeating Monsters

  • When you give damage to the Wight King while in an Order, your Order contribution level will rise; however if you leave your Order it will reset.
  • Only Order members with an Order Contribution level of at least 100,000 can receive these rewards.
  • Also, 'Order Rewards for Defeating Monsters' cannot be received when in a different Order.

Event Battles[edit]

  • Battles in this event will consume ATK PWR.
  • One battle reduces ATK PWR by: 5 + (consumed deck ATK PWR / 2)
  • During event battles, only special event skills will be launched.
  • 'Realm Matching' and 'Morale' boost effects will not be active in this event.
  • Please refrain from tapping the screen repeatedly, for it could cause problems with the animations.

Co-op Requests[edit]

  • Selecting 'Call for More Fellows / Members' will automatically send your request for help.
  • You cannot ask any Allies (Fellows/Order members) for help who are already in a battle with a different monster.

Individual Ranking (Total Damage) Rewards[edit]

  • The Total Damage Ranking ranks all participants in the event by their total damage to monsters.
  • Changes to rankings may take about 30 minutes to display.
  • The actual number of attacks or monsters defeated are not shown in the ranking.
  • When there is a tie, the one that accomplishes the record faster will get a better rank.
  • Final ranking will be calculated at the end of the event.

Order Ranking (Total Damage) Rewards[edit]

  • Total Damage / Attacks will be added to the Order the person belongs to the moment he or she attacks.
  • These Total Damage / Attacks will not be carried over to other Orders when you switch Orders during the event.
  • Note: during the event, if you transfer to another Order with a lower ranking you will not receive the same reward. If you leave your Order before the event ends, you will not receive Order rewards.
  • Rewards will be distributed to Orders after the event.

End of Event[edit]

  • Monsters will disappear at the end of the event.
  • Note: the schedule of the event may change without warning.
  • Notifications concerning when the rewards will be distributed will be sent to you as soon as possible. If you leave your Order before the event ends, you will not receive Order rewards.
