New Features[edit]

Empowered Raid bosses
When both Pandemonium Mode and Bahamut's Favor are active, you may encounter an empowered version of the rarest raid boss. This raid boss has 6 billion HP, and drops significantly more items than other raid bosses.
An empowered raid boss may be attacked by a maximum of 200 users. It will disappear from all other users' Raid Boss list when the maximum is reached.


Wounded Raid Bosses
During the event quest, you may encounter raid bosses that have already been attacked by other users (as if you were called to help defeat it), instead of one with full HP. These raid bosses go under Others's Discoveries in your list.
The original discoverer of such a raid boss remains unchanged, and will receive Discoverer rewards upon the defeat of the raid boss.


Your Cryoknight[edit]

  • Train your Cryoknight using the tomes and spheres that raid bosses drop when defeated.
  • Users who train the your Cryoknight a lot will get a special Legend card at the end of the event. Users who didn't will still get a significantly weaker SS Rare card, but it won't be as good. Cryoknight's exact stats, alignment, and evolution depend on the type and number of items used in training.


Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours.

  • One Elixir recovers 1 AP during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the Dragoon's Dilemma page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.
