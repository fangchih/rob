About[edit]

With each purchase comes a bonus item!

Get step items to move up and improve the quality of bonus items!

Among the bonus items is a claim ticket which might drop an event Legend card. the drop rate of the ticket increases as you purchase more packs!
Get the step item four times on Step 5 and get a special Legend of your choice!

Notes[edit]

  • You will get one bonus item with each purchase of the card pack. After you get a bonus item, the item will be removed from the possible five bonus items. One step can be purchased a total of five times
  • If you get the step item from among the bonus items, you'll be able to move up a step. Tap the Step Up button to go even further!
  • If you have already gotten all of the bonus items from a step, you will have to go to the next cycle before you can purchase the card pack again. Tap the Next Cycle button to go even further!
  • Bonus items change as you step up. you cannot revert to a previous step after you have stepped up.
  • Getting the step item on each of the five steps counts as a cycle. You'll be able to purchase four cycles a day.
  • After you finish four cycles, the card pack will change. The card pack will include one Event L 7% Drop Rate card pack claim ticket and 15 holy powders. This pack will be available until the card pack is reset.
  • The Wellspring card pack resets daily at 5 AM (ET). You will be returned to Step 1 regardless of the step you were on previously.
  • Card pack claim tickets that you get from the Wellspring card pack must be used by 8 PM Dec. 25th, 2013 (ET)
