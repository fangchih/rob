A[edit]

  • AE - Ancient Elf
  • Alb - Alberich
  • Aps - Apsara
  • AQ - Angel Queen
  • Arch - Archdaemon
  • ArchQ - Archangel Queen
  • Ark - Ark Dragon
  • Alt - alternative account. A account next to the main account.
  • Ax - Ax Master

B[edit]

  • B / B> - Buying
  • baha - Bahamut
  • base atk/def - the base atk of your atk deck and the base def of your def deck. Base atk/def are shown in the deck menu.
  • bm - Bloody Mistress
  • BR - Battle Rankings
  • brb - be right back, chat slang
  • Bunny - Bunny Lilith Limil and Angel Bunny
  • Buc - Buccaneer
  • BZ - Bazaar

C[edit]

  • Camu - Camulia
  • Cerb - Cerberus
  • Chim - Chimaera
  • Clay - Claymore Maid
  • CP - Card Packs
  • CW - Cure Water
  • Cyb - Cybele

D[edit]

  • Date - Date Masamune
  • DD - Dark Dragoon
  • DG - Dark General
  • DQ - Devil Queen
  • DP - Devil Princess

E[edit]

  • evo - refers to how the card has been evolved to its final form. Example: 6-11 evo

F[edit]

  • Feeder - Feedercard or feeder account. Feeder account is an 2nd account which has every point put into stamina.
  • FCP - Friendship Card Pack
  • FF - Final Form of a card. It cannot be evolved any further.
  • FP - Friendship Points and the card Fairy Princess

G[edit]

  • gd - Gold Dragon
  • gfox - Gold Fox
  • gilg - Gilgamesh
  • gs - Great Spirit
  • guin - Guinevere

H[edit]

  • HN - High Normal card
  • Homu/Homo -Elder Homunculus
  • HP - Holy Powder
  • HW - Holy War

I[edit]

  • IGN - ingame name = player name in RoB.
  • Ilma - Ilmatar
  • Inu - Inugami

J[edit]

  • Jorm - Jormungand

K[edit]

  • k - means "okay" in chat slang. But in combination with rupies, "k" refers to 1000 ("thousand") like it does in kilogram = 1000 gram. Examples: 1k is 1000 rupies, 100k is 100 000 rupies. 1kk = 1 million = 1 000 000 rupies.
  • ka - King Arthur

L[edit]

  • Lance - Lancelot
  • LCP - Legendary Card Pack
  • LE - Lovely Elf
  • LF - looking for
  • Liz - Lilith Liz
  • Liml - Bunny Lilith Limil
  • Longi - Longinus
  • Luci / Lucy - Dark Knight Luciella

M[edit]

  • Maid - Claymore Maid
  • Manit - Manticore
  • msam / msammy - Master Samurai
  • MAX - refers to a card which has been enhanced to its maximum level.
  • Maya - Mayahuel
  • MC - Magic Circle
  • Melu - Melusine
  • mg / Mgunner - Master Gunner
  • mgolem - Mythril Golem
  • Mino - Minotaur
  • Mike - Michael
  • mw / mWitch - Master Witch

N[edit]

  • NM - Nightmare
  • Nobu - Nobunaga the Ruthless

O[edit]

  • OBO - or best offer
  • Ody - Odysseus

P[edit]

  • Pally/Pala - Paladin
  • PHP - Holy Powder (Personal)
  • Pop - Master Pop Star
  • Princy - Principality

R[edit]

  • R - Rare card
  • Rainbow (deck) - A deck with cards from different realms mixed up. It doesn't get realm bonus for every card. Strong at legend deck level.
  • Raph - Raphaella

S[edit]

  • S / S> - Selling
  • Sammy - Master Samurai
  • Sig / Siggy - Siegmund
  • SR - Super Rare card
  • SSR - Super Super Rare card
  • SL - S Legend (in Japanese version)
  • Suc - Succubus
  • Sut / Suth - Sutekh
  • Svalk / sword valk - Sword Valkyrie

T[edit]

  • tia - Tiamat
  • treasure HR/R - refers to the Treasure Cards, that you get from collecting a complete set of treasures. HR and R is the cards rarity.
  • trist - Tristan
  • trow - Trow Elf
  • TV - Temptress Vampire

V[edit]

  • valk - Valkyrie Hild

W[edit]

  • wc - refers to 2 different cards: Witch Commander and Witchcrafter
  • wing - wing card, refers to the 4th and 5th slot in a deck. They are called wing cards, because in the battle display, their pictures will be shown on the left (4th) and right (5th).
  • WTB - Want to buy
  • WTS - Want to sell
  • WTT - Want to trade


X[edit]

  • X Legend - eXtraordinary Legend

Y[edit]

  • Yuki - Sanada Yukimura

Z[edit]

  • Zerk / Zerker - Berserker

0–9[edit]

  • 4max - evolving with 4 base cards and max all cards: 4-7
  • 6max - evolving with 6 base cards and max all cards: 6-11
  • 8max - evolving with 8 base cards and max all cards: 8-15
  • 9HN - 9 High Normal Feeders, those can be base High Normals or Normals evolved to High Normals. Used to level up a card until MAX level (maturity).
  • 9HN skilled - 9 High Normal Feeders which have a skill. Used for skill leveling a card.
  • 9N - 9 Normal Feeders. Used to level up a card until MAX level (maturity).
