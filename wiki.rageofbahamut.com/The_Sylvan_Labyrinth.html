Story[edit]

A beautiful forest full of elves...
was suddenly enshrouded by darkness.
Then the demons appeared...
What could wait in the depths of this sylvan labyrinth?


How to Play this Event[edit]

Create a deck of 10 cards to attack the Creeping Corruption who appeared without warning.

When you defeat all the monsters the Gates of Hell will open and a rare boss will appear!

Defeat bosses in cooperation with Allies to get rewards.

Detailed event rules[edit]

  1. Seek monsters in quests!
    • Monsters appear in quests!
    • When you battle more monsters, it's more likely that powerful enemies will appear!
  2. Seek out rare boss monsters!
    • Spend AP to attack monsters!
    • Send a Co-op Request to call Allies after you attack.
  3. How to deal major damage
    • Spend ATK PWR to deal lots of damage!
    • Save up points on the Break Gauge to activate Rising Break!
  4. Fight effectively when Skills are active!
    • Decks are made up of 3 Units (4 cards, 3 cards and 3 cards).
    • Only the Skills of Unit Leaders will activate!
  5. Collect Sylvan Berries to rank high!
    • Get Sylvan Berries when you defeat a monster!
    • Collect Sylvan Berries to rank high!

Reset Box[edit]

For Blue/Silver Maze Flowers box:

  • When you get Kaguya, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.

For Red/Gold Maze Flowers box:

  • When you get Elven Queen, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.

Caution

  • Even if the Results screen is not displayed due to an error, the item could still have been sent normally. Please check Presents to see whether you received your items.
  • If you have drawn all of the contents in the box, you will not receive a Present.
