About this event[edit]

A new individual event to pass the time and earn some items, Rupies, HP, CW, cards, CP Tickets, and Friendship Points WHILE at the same time earning Honor Points.

You earn Stamps of Excellence which can be exchanged for Holy Powder (Personal) at the end of the event.
Each stamp earns you 1 Holy Powder (Personal).
Earn 15 Stamps and get an additional 3 Holy Powder (Personal) as a bonus.


Battle[edit]

You must battle by clicking the Hermit in the Hood icon/graphic, then clicking on Battle.

If you don't, you will not earn Azurite Crystals nor your Stamps.

How to Reset the Crucible[edit]

When you perform alchemy, the crucible turns azurite crystals into a card or other item. Each crucible is able to produce a specific number of specific items and cards.

You'll be able to reset the crucible when it produces Devil Queen. The crucible can be reset a maximum of three times.

Caution

  • If the Results screen does not appear when you perform alchemy, please check the Presents screen. It's likely that you received your rewards without issue.
  • A crucible becomes unusable once it has produced all possible cards and items.
