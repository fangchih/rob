Differences from Last Time[edit]

  • New, higher title to strive for! (Crafty Baron)
  • Vice leaders get a new spell, Untapped Rage. Athena's Blessing will now be the support leaders' spell!
  • A new Boost feature will use ATK/DEF PWR to raise your ATK/DEF.
  • The Match and Order Forum screens have been revamped.
  • There are many new rewards for sacrificing souls.
  • New Castle Crusher cards have double skills.
  • The Attack and Recover processes have been streamlined.
  • Order positions can no longer be changed during battle.
