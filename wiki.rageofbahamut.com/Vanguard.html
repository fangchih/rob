Story[edit]

Legends tell of a goddess who stands at the wheel of fate.
Each time she turns the wheel, the course of someone's life changes.
The High Fencer sought to uncover her destiny
in an ancient and mystical forest.
None could have anticipated what lay in wait for her...

Card Pack Details[edit]

  • Tap one of the nine circles to draw a Vanguard card pck. Each circle can only be tapped once.
  • There are a set number of goddess circles in each area:

- Area 1: 2 circles

- Area 2: 3 circles

- Area 3 and beyond: 4 circles

  • When you discover all of the goddess circles in an area, you will move to the next area.
  • Once you advance, you cannot return to earlier areas.
  • Goddess circles drop a bonus card: Fortuna, Gareth, Mushussu, High Fencer or a Legend card. Legends have a 10 times drop rate!
