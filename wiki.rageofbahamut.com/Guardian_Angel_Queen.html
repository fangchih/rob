Sells for: 1 R


Source:
Welcome Back Campaign

Also obtained from:



This card cannot be Traded, Gifted or sold at the Bazaar. Currently you can't evolve it, but you'll be able to evolve it at a later date.

When used has an enhancer card it will give 300% experience, meaning it will raise the card level by 3 for each Guardian Angel Queen used, not to exceed the card's maximum level.

When lucky, you get an excellent enhancement which grants a 150% boost to the experience gained, making the boost 450%
