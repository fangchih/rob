Time Period (ET): 9 PM Jun. 12th, 2014 to 12 AM Jun. 17th, 2014

Prime Time Draw Period (ET): 4 AM Jun. 18th, 2014

Details[edit]

Buy a pack, get up to 30 free cards! Drop rates jump for bonus draws!

Plus Event cards as perks!

High Rare - Legend card drop rates boosted by 1.5 times! Special cards have their drop rates boosted greatly!

You will receive points when you go into Prime Time based on the drop rate you get.

  • Cards that you draw in Prime Time will not result in points.

Notes[edit]

  • Prime Time is a special mode that gives you free card pack draws with boosted SR-L drop rates.
  • When Prime Time occurs in the 1-card pack, your SR-L drop rate will rise and you'll get one, two or three free draws.
  • When Prime Time occurs in the 10-card pack, your SR-L drop rate will rise and you'll get 10-30 free draws.
  • Prime Time ends when all of its free draws have been used. After that, the Prime card pack will return to its normal form.
  • If you draw four card packs and Prime Time hasn't occurred yet, it will occur with a 10 times SR-L drop rate on the fifth draw.
  • Whenever Prime Time occurs, the number of draws left until you are guaranteed to get a SR-L 10 times drop rate draw will reset.
  • After the Prime card pack has ended, there will be a Prime Time exchange period during which you can use your Prime Time draws if you have any. Once this period has passed, you will no longer be able to use Prime Time draws.

Point Notes[edit]

  • You will receive points when you go into Prime Time based on the drop rate you get.
  • Free draws during Prime Time don't give you points.
  • The 2,700 MobaCoin/RageMedal tier is only available if you have fewer than 274 points.
  • If you reach 280 points, both price tiers will become unavailable even if the time period hasn't ended yet. If you get over 280 points, your points will be rounded down to 280.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different form the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
