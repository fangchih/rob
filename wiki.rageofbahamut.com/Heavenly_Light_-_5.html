About Heavenly Light Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

The first pack is free for everyone!

20% Legend Ticket after 30 draws!

Notes[edit]

  • Up to 30 packs can be drawn.
  • The first draw is free, the second draw is 100 RageMedals, and all additional draws are 300 RageMedals.
  • Card pack tickets will be sent to your Presents.
  • Drop rate boosts are randomly selected.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
