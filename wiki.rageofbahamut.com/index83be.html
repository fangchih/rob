Tapping your dragon on the main page "Tyr is grunting. It seems to want your attention."

Tapping the Dragon Icon shows/hides the level and name bar at the top.

Skills -- Maximum skill level is 10. There are Inherited and Uninherited skills. Learned skills are based on the dragon's type.

First checked at level 2, Skill Points: 5/60 "The dragon acquires one skill point each time it evolves without the use of an item."


Embryonic stone -- Skill points are not lost upon use, only taken off of the skills. Only one uninherited skill is kept upon use. You would have to make the same type of dragon to get the same skills. This is useful for choosing and accumulating up to six inherited skills for your dragon.

Re: -- notes on the dragon -- Lucasmembrane (talk) 21:05, 1 July 2014 (EDT)

I've been digging myself... The adult dragon evolves at levels 15 and 40. The experience needed to reach the next level is (300 + [cur.level x 10]), so reaching lv 15 from a level 1 adult takes 5690 exp, lv 40 takes 20190, and lv 50 takes 26940. Reaching level 50 does in fact grant a gem that goes in the monolith... I still don't know what happens when you get all three, because the embryonic shards are only available every 72 hours, so I'm stuck with only two of the gems till Thursday :P

The skills seem to be similar for every dragon. Lv 5 is a castle crusher skill, lv 50 is a raid event skill, and lv 10, 20, and 30 are arena skills that give a boost if you have good cards from a particular realm (fire=demon, water=man, wind=gods). I'll add the actual skill text and images for the two I've done (wind and water) to the wiki sometime soon :)
