Changes to Holy War Battles[edit]

Opposing Order Holy War Point Reduction[edit]

  • While ragnarok is active, if you win a battle, you'll reduce your opposing order's holy war points by 5% (not 20%) of the holy war points you gain from that battle. However, you will still get 1.5 times the usual holy war points if you win a whole war.


Critical Hits and Devastating Blows[edit]

  • Once in a while, you might deal a critical hit or devastating blow to your opponent.
  • When you deal a critical hit to an opponent, 10% of the holy war points you earn will be taken from your opponent's order. When you deal a devastating blow, this increases to 20%.
  • You must win a battle for a critical hit and devastating blow to affect the holy war points of your opponent's order.
  • If opponent has zero holy war points Critical Hits and Devasting Blows will have NO affect to opponent order's holy war points.

* The normal 5% hit to your opponent's holy war points will not take effect in battles where you deal a critical hit or devastating blow.

Changes to the ATK Leader Skill Warcry[edit]

  • It is possible to deal full-force attacks when warcry is active.
  • When warcry is active, you will always be able to deal a devastating blow.
