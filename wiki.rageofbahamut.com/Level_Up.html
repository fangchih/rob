About CP Levels[edit]

When your CP level advances, the S Rare/SS Rare drop rate is enhanced!

Get an S Rare or up for an opportunity to skip ahead to even better CPs. Receive an S Rare or SS Rare, you'll jump up to the Guaranteed SR & Up CP!

Below are the conditions that advance or reset your level:


Enhanced Rare & Up Drop Rate CP

  • Level Up Requirements (conditions to advance from this level): Get a Rare or High Rare and you're guaranteed to advance to the SR/SSR 3x Drop Rate CP!
  • Receive an S Rare or SS Rare and you'll jump to the Guaranteed SR & Up CP!


SR/SSR 3x Drop Rate CP

  • Level Up Requirements (conditions to advance from this level): Get a Rare for an opportunity to advance! Receive a High Rare or up and you're guaranteed to move ahead!
  • You'll have an opportunity to jump ahead to the Guaranteed SR & Up CP when you get an S Rare or SS Rare!
  • No level reset conditions apply.


SR/SSR 4x Drop Rate CP

  • Level Up Requirements (conditions to advance from this level): Get a Rare or High Rare for an opportunity to advance. Receive an S Rare or up and you're guaranteed to move ahead!
  • You'll have an opportunity to jump ahead to the Guaranteed SR & Up CP when you get an S Rare or SS Rare!
  • No level reset conditions apply.


SR/SSR 5x Drop Rate CP

  • Level Up Requirements (conditions to advance from this level): Get a Rare or High Rare for an opportunity to advance. Receive an S Rare or up and you're guaranteed to move ahead!
  • You'll have an opportunity to jump ahead to the Guaranteed SR & Up CP when you get an S Rare or SS Rare!
  • No level reset conditions apply.


SR/SSR 10x Drop Rate CP

  • All cards in the CP are Rare and up!
  • Level Up Requirements (conditions to advance from this level): Get a High Rare for an opportunity to advance to the next CP.
  • Level Reset Conditions (conditions that reset your level): If you receive an S Rare or SS Rare, your level may reset.


Guaranteed SR & Up CP

  • Loop Requirements (conditions to stay in this leel): You'll be able to get this CP again when you get an SS Rare! Receive an SR, and you might enter the loop!
  • Level Reset Conditions (conditions that reset your level): When you get an S Rare, your level may reset.


Reset Bonuses

  • You'll get an additional bonus if your level resets from the SR/SSR 10x Drop Rate CP level.
  • If your level resets, you'll get a Reset Bonus of 2 extra bonus items with each of your next 10 Level Up CPs after that.
  • If your CP level resets again while you still have Reset Bonuses available, you will be able to get 10 more Reset Bonuses. (i.e. When your CP level resets, your Reset Bonus Count starts at 10. If you get 3 more Level Up CPs, you'll get 3 Reset Bonuses and the count will go down to 7. If your CP level resets at that time, your Reset Bonus Count will go up to 17.)

Caution[edit]

Rarities and requirements differ according to CP level. In some cases, depending on the CP level, you will not level up to the next CP even when you get a card that fulfills requirements. When your CP level is reset, you will be returned to the Enhanced Rare and Up Drop Rate CP.

Note: Moving to the next level is not guaranteed on any pack, even if you receive a card that is a necessary condition for moving to the next level (in some cases necessary cards are insufficient for moving to the next level.)
