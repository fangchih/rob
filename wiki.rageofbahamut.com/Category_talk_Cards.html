For kicks the 8max formula was (atk1*1.8+(maxatk1-atk1)*1.8+0.2*(atk1*1.4+(maxatk1-atk1)*1.4+0.2*(atk1*1.2+(maxatk1-atk1)*1.2+0.2(maxatk1)))) and as you can see there's a lot of cancellation, but that won't be the case with other evolution though we could simplify to (X*atk1+Y*maxatk1) of some sort. I modified Template:Cardset test and verified with Principality test and the calculation matched the calc.
