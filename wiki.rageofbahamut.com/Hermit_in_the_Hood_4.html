About this event[edit]

Battle for Crystals, Then Alchemize Them!

Collect Azurite Crystals, Get Stamps!

Each Stamp you get is worth one personal holy powder at the end of the event. Collect twelve stamps for three bonus personal holy powders. You can earn up to 3 stamps a day.


Notes
  • New stamp sheets are distributed every day at 5 AM (ET). Similarly, the number of azurite crystals obtained in battle is counted from 5 AM (ET).
  • Your total number of azurite crystals will not be reset until the end of the event.


Battle[edit]

You must battle by clicking the Hermit in the Hood icon/graphic, then clicking on Battle.

If you don't, you will not earn Azurite Crystals nor Stamps.
