How to Play

Join a six-user team and form a brigade!

Fight bosses and compete to collect the most ranking shards!

Use three sapphire seals to spawn a strong raid boss!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more ranking shards drops than other brigades.
  • This event will have eight daily 22-hour campaigns. The number of ranking shards that brigades collect is calulated per campaign.
  • If you don't create or join a team by 7 PM Apr. 29th, 2015 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.


New Features

Double Strike
  • Double Strikes use two powders at once to attack a raid boss. They deal more damage than two seperate single-powder attacks.
    • Holy Powder double strikes deal 17 times more damage than a 1-AP attack.
    • Ultimate Powder double strikes deal 34 times more damage than a 1-AP attack.
  • The option to make double strikes isn't available for all bosses.
  • Powders can't be mixed.
Campaign Achievments
Completing achievments in each campaign will enable one-off and passive effects. These effects add bonus shards to brigade total
ranking shards in each campaign. These bonuses do not add to user's individual ranking shards. There are two categories of achievments; normal and cooperative.
  • Normal
Unlocked by defeating raid bosses. Each type of raid boss has its own set of acchievments,
and the passive effects may trigger only when raid bosses of the respective type are defeated.
  • Cooperative
Unlocked by defeating raid bosses where there are at least two participants in the battle. Each type of raid boss has its own set of achievments,
and the passive effects may trigger only when raid bosses of the respective type are defeated by at least two participants.
Achievments are reset at the beginning of each campaign

Daily Event Bonus

Elixirs, Precious Rocks, and Exalted Sapphires are available for free on the event screen once every day

  • Each Elixir recovers 1 AP used to attack raid bosses
  • Precious Rocks and Exalted Sapphires can be exchanged for rewards.
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed once every 24 hours, starting from 6 AM (ET).
