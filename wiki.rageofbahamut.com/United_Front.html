About Triplefate Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Purchase card packs to collect Unity Points and

work together to light up the candles to get more rewards!

Unity Point Reward Details[edit]

Notes[edit]

  • Candles lit based on the total amount of Unity Points collected by all users.
  • Users will receive additional rewards for each Unity Point held by each individual user. These rewards will be determined by the number of candles lit, when this card pack expires.
  • This card pack can only be drawn ten times.
  • The additional rewards will be automatically sent to Presents within 24 hours after this card pack expires.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
