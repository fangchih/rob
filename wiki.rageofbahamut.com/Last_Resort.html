How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade! Tame devil monkeys for thermae buns and geyser tickets. Alpha devil monkeys yield the most!

  • In this event, you'll compete in brigades made of five teams of six users each to see which brigade can collect more amenta shards during a campaign.
  • This event will have seven daily 19-hour campaigns.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 9 PM Apr. 22nd, 2014 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Zesty Drinks and commoner geyser tickets are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting at the beginning of your campaign.

  • One zesty drink recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Commoner geyser tickets can be exchanged for rewards. On the Sands of Awakening screen, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.

All brigades have a chance at the extra killer completion, no matter how many campaigns they win or lose.
