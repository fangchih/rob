Differences from the last Holy Wars[edit]

There are many changes to the first Holy War of 2013, Wrath of Lightning. Position Skills being the most drastic. The following list of changes are planned for this Holy Wars event:

Holy Wars Preparation Period (ET) Jan 11th, 2013 - Jan, ?, 2013 (Probably Jan 17th, when the Excellent Enhancement Campaign ends, like last holy war)

Major Change[edit]

Previously, a member of your opposing Order had to defeat one of your DEF Leaders in order to be able to attack the rest of your members. However, the new rules state that a DEF Leader will only gain the ability above when using the Phalanx Position Skill. Therefore the following:

  • When Phalanx Position Skill is in effect: The DEF Leader that used this skill will draw all attacks from the opponent's Order members. The opponent will need to defeat that DEF Leader to attack the rest of the members.
  • When Phalanx Position Skill is NOT in effect: All members can attack any opposing Order members.

In essence, you DO NOT need to take out the DEF Leader UNTIL he uses his Position Skill.

How to use skills:[edit]

  • Attack another user to recover SP (SP will recover based on ATK PWR used)
  • Tap the 'Charge' button to recover SP / ATK PWR / DEF PWR (Possible every 20 minutes)
  • Once you have enough SP, tap the launch button to use a skill (Possible whenever SP recovers)
  • SP resets after every war - Use It Or Loose It - before the war ends. It does not carry over to the next war.

Other Changes[edit]

Holy War Point Skill Cards[edit]

  • Skills of Fully-evolved Luminous Knight, Chiron and High Werewolf


The fully-evolved forms of Luminous Knight, Chiron and High Werewolf have stronger Skills than their forms, providing a 'Great boost.' When their Skills reach LVL 10, they provide at most 25% boost.* Although it was previously announced that those Skill Effects would be reduced from 'Great boost' to 'Big boost' in Holy Wars IX and onward, they will actually continue to be 'Great boost' in Holy Wars IX. (After Holy Wars IX, those Skill Effects will be reduced to 'Big boost.')

Position Skill[edit]

  1. Each Position in an Order will have an advantageous Skill in Holy Wars.
  2. You consume SP (Skill Points) when you use these skills.
  3. SP will be raised based on the amount of ATK PWR used at the beginning of the Battle. The amount raised will not be affected by the outcome of the Battle.
    • You can also raise your SP through the 'Charge' button or ally skills.
  4. Depending on your Position, your max SP, SP required to use your Skill and amount recovered via the Charge button will vary.

Position LVL[edit]

  1. Each Position has an associated LVL.
  2. Your Position LVL rises up to 5, based on Holy War Points gained.
  3. Each Position requires a different number of Holy War Points to level-up.
  4. If your Position LVL rises, your Position Skill's effectivesness will also rise.
  5. If you change Positions but then go back to your old Position, you'll start again from your old Position LVL. (e.g. a LVL 2 ATK Leader who becomes a DEF Leader and then ATK Leader will start again at LVL 2.)
  6. Your Position LVL will reset at the end of every Holy Wars event.
About Positions[edit]
  1. The Support Leader Position has been added to Orders.

Event Restrictions[edit]

  1. During Holy War events, Order Leaders will be able to change other members' Positions.
  2. However, Positions can't be changed between a War Declaration and the 1st battle of a Holy War.

Chains[edit]

  1. If you win a Battle and win another within 5 minutes, a Chain will start.
  2. Effects of Chain Bonus:
    • While Chain Bonus is active ATK will be boosted by 10%.
    • The number of the Chain = more Holy War Points (up to a 50% boost).
  3. Holy War Point Bonus from Chain Bonus:
    • If the same Order member adds to the chain, you will get a 1% bonus.
    • If a different Order member adds to the chain, you will get a 2% bonus.
  4. Even if you lose a Battle, the Chain will continue.
  5. The Chain will end if it is not refreshed within 5 minutes.
  6. The Chain can only reach a set number.

Full-force Attacks[edit]

  1. In Holy Wars, you can perform a Full-force Attack by using Holy Powder.
  2. When using Full-force Attack, all of your ATK PWR will be consumed, regardless of the ATK PWR COST of your deck.
  3. You can use either 3 Holy Powders or 1 Holy Powder when you perform a Full-force Attack.
  4. Rules for Full-force Attack:
    • Decks:
      • The deck used will be your selected deck
      • If there is no deck selected, the Recommended ATK Deck will be used
    • Holy War Point Calculation:
      • The amount of Holy War Points accumulated will be based on the full amount of ATK PWR consumed, not the ATK PWR COST of the deck used.
      • If you consume 3 Holy Powders when you battle, you can accumulate points equivalent to the total amount of ATK PWR consumed times 3.
      • You can exceed the Holy War Point limit.
  5. If you lose the Battle, you do not accumulate any Holy War Points.
  6. Only 1 Win or Loss will be counted regardless of how many Holy Powders were used.

Changes to Holy War Points Received[edit]

  1. Higher minimum Holy War Points gained when attacking users of a lower LVL.
  2. Higher minimum Holy War Points gained when attacking users with lower DEF PWR.
  3. Different Holy War Point bonus when beating users with Positions:
    • 120% Holy War Points gained for beating the Order Leader; 110% for beating other members with Positions. (Still no bonus for beating regular members)

Silver Coin Card Packs[edit]

  1. Silver Coin CPs will be divided into 'Unlimited Cards' and 'Mystery Box Cards,' which you can draw from Mystery Boxes. (Cards won't always be drawn from Mystery Boxes.)
  2. You'll get up to 300 Silver Coins from one Holy War.

Ad Hoc Orders[edit]

  • If you don't belong to an Order by the time the Holy Wars event starts, you'll be added to an Ad Hoc Order automatically. (This is limited to users who meet the requirements to join Ad Hoc Orders.)

What are Ad Hoc Orders?

  • Ad Hoc Orders are formed automatically during Holy Wars event periods.
  • These are formed with users who aren't in Orders.
  • Users under LVL 20 can't join Ad Hoc Orders.
  • Only users who registered their devices can join Ad Hoc Orders.
  • Even if you are invited to a regular Order, an Ad Hoc Order will form first.
  • Once a Holy War event starts, you won't be able to invite others to your Order.
  • If you have an Order you want to join, make sure to do so before Holy Wars starts!
  • Ad Hoc Orders disband when Holy Wars events end.
  • In Ad Hoc Orders there are no Order Leaders or Vice-Leaders. Leaders are assigned automatically to users in the order that they join the Order.
  1. ATK Leader
  2. DEF Leader
  3. onward: Regular Member (not a Leader)
  • Each Ad Hoc Order has a maximum of 25 members.

In Ad Hoc Orders, You Can:

  • Declare war
  • Donate
  • Build Walls
  • Rupies donated or Walls built do not carry over into other Orders.
  • Ad Hoc Order names are created automatically and can't be changed.
  • Please understand that Ad Hoc Order specs may change without prior notice.

Order Victory Reward[edit]

You can earn rewards depending on the number of Holy Wars your Order wins. So cooperate together with your Order members to get the most wins and the most rewards!


Gold Rewards

  • Order Victories: 50+
  • Reward: Diana (S Rare) x 2
  • Cruel Priestess (High Rare) x 1

Only Order members with at least 30,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Gold Rewards. Order members with 5,000-29,999 Points will receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 Points will receive Bronze Rewards.


Silver Rewards

  • Order Victories: 30-49
  • Reward: Diana (S Rare) x 1
  • Cruel Priestess (High Rare) x 1

Only Order members with at least 5,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 Points will receive Bronze Rewards.


Bronze Rewards

  • Order Victories: 10-29
  • Reward: Cruel Priestess (High Rare) x 1

Only Order members with at least 1,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Bronze Rewards.


Order members with fewer than 1,000 Holy War Points will not receive this reward, regardless of their Order's number of Holy War victories.
Order Victories refer to an Order's number of Holy Wars won, not an individual user's number of battles won.
