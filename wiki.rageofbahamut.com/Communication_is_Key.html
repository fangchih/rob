Communication is Key

From Rage of Bahamut WIKI
Jump to: navigation, search

In most events - especially Onslaught - communication is key. This game is primarily a "Social" game; yet, the communication through the Rage of Bahamut game can be tedious and time consuming. The best Orders take advantage of the communication tools out there to: 1) Organize events such as Onslaught battles; 2) Communicate the Order's rules and goals; and 3) Foster a feeling of camaraderie.

When choosing your Order's communication platform, you'll want to insure that it's easy to install, easy to use, and allows for privacy (you don't want just anyone in the group chat).

Some of the most popular chat platforms are listed below in no particular order.

Line[edit]

Pros:

  • Create or join groups as you wish and chat with up to 100 people at once.
  • Available on PC, so you can battle AND coordinate your attacks without interrupting game play.
  • Available on both Android and Apple devices.

Cons:

  • You'll need a phone number to register the app.
  • Although the Group Bulletin board is a nice function, it currently cannot be read through the PC interface.


Line website: http://line.naver.jp/en/
Download the app using the QR code

  • Pretty much the same as Line with Private or Public chat groups.
  • Available on PC, so you can battle AND coordinate your attacks without interrupting game play.
