Changes from Past Holy Wars[edit]

The following changes are planned for this Holy Wars event.

For more general information about Holy Wars in general, please see our Holy Wars page.


Holy War Schedule[edit]

The Holy Wars schedule has been changed. View the new schedule and participate in the carnage!


Declare War!
Orders choose when to start fighting.
Fight up to 4 wars in a 24 hour period.


Start Fighting!
Wars last 60 minutes each.
Once a war ends, prepare for the next one!



Holy War Card Pack[edit]

The exploration cards, Master Mercenary, Angelic Shooter, and Annerose are available from the holy war card pack!

Wars are scheduled in advance, one war at a time. Plan ahead!

Declare War in Advance
  • Every war is one hour long.
  • Orders will now decide on specific times that they decide to declare war.
  • Only the order leader and vice-leader choose when to declare war.
Any user can set or change war times in ad hoc orders.
  • You can declare war or change the time of a war up to 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • You cannot declare war two times back-to-back.
  • You cannot declare a future war while you're fighting a war.
  • You can declare 4 total wars in a 24-hour period from 10:00 PM (ET) daily. You will be able to enter a maximum of 16 wars during the event period.

Victory Rewards[edit]

An order gets 1 victory point every time it wins a war. When Holy Wars ends, orders receive rewards according to how many victory points they collected. These victory point rewards can only be collected by users who meet the individual holy war point requirements.

Victory Points: 13+
Rewards:

  • Holy Wars XIV: As One HW SR Exchange Ticket x 2
  • Bandit King (High Rare) x 2
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 4

Only order members with at least 30,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 16+ victory points. Members with 5,000 - 29,999 points will get the reward for 9-12 victory points. Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 5-8 victory points.


Victory Points: 9-12
Rewards:

  • Holy Wars XIV: As One HW SR Exchange Ticket x 1
  • Gruesome Demon (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 2

Only order members with at least 5,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 9-12 victory points. Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 5-8 victory points.


Victory Points: 5-8
Rewards:

  • Gruesome Demon (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 1

Only order members with at least 1,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 8-11 victory points.
