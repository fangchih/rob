Story[edit]

Evil permeates the air
The demonic castle awaits
Filled with vampires in wait.
The nearby village lies empty,
Sucked dry by demonic fiends.
Vampire hunters descend.
Purifying in His name.
A knight and vampireling
Approach the crossroad of memories.


How to Play This Event[edit]

Proceed through the quest and root out the vampires!
Succeed in purification to get rewards!!
Battle vampire nobles with a 5-card deck!
Accumulate banishing points and aim for the top!


Event Participation Bonus[edit]

Every day of the Memories of the Dark event, when you go to the event screen Vania gives you Radiant Incense and Crimson Tear.

  • Radiant Incense recover Stamina, which you need to proceed through the event.
  • Crimson Tear are exchangeable for rewards.
  • The event participation bonus is reset daily at 6 AM (ET).



Special Notes[edit]

Stamina consumption in this event for replays of quests on level 50 is scaled proportional to your stamina. This means that if you increase your stamina from, say, 266 points to 269 Points, it goes up from 9 points per try to 10 points per try - disproportionally PUNISHING the player for improving that stat, as you can now quest only 26 times instead of 29 times, and have to wait longer per try to boot.

First and Second Halves[edit]

Once the first half of the event is over, you will get an individual rewards based on your interim rankings!

Special cards to help you through the second half of the event might be available too!

Your rank after the event ends will be based on your total ranking through both halves of the event!
