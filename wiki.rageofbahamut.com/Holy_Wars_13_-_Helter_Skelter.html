Exploring[edit]

You can start exploring as soon as a war starts!
  • You'll be able to explore your surroundings during wars.
  • By using stamina, you'll be able to proceed through different areas which each have various items that are useful in wars.
  • The drop rates of items depend on which area you explore.


Items Found by Exploring

  • Essence of Encouragement: This recovers some of your SP. * If the order leader gets this while shingeki is active, it won't recover their SP.
  • Essence of Loyalty: This recovers some of the order leader's SP. * It wont' recover their SP while shingeki is active.
  • Essence of Enigmas: This recovers some of your ATK PWR.
  • Horn of Warriors: This gives a small boost to holy war points.
  • Horn of Lords: This gives a big boost to holy war points.
  • Horn of the Lion: This gives a great boost to holy war points.
  • Statue of War: This gives all members of your order an ATK boost for a certain period of time. While one is active, getting a second and third one will boost your ATK even further. Getting a fourth one will make the boost last longer.
  • Statue of Destruction: This gives all members of your order an ATK boost against walls/castles for a certain period of time. While one is active, getting a second and third one will boost your ATK even further. Getting a fourth one will make the boost last longer.
  • Blessed Ring: If you have any of this item when you win a battle, one will be used to give a 5% boost to the holy war points that you achieve then. You can have up to 99 at a time.

All Items except for blessed rings will be used as soon as you get them. They cannot be saved in your item list. Blessed Rings do carry over into your next war.

If you have a special Holy Wars card that boosts exploring drop rates, you must add it in the Explore menu in order for its skill to take effect.

The Holy Wars schedule has been changed. View the new schedule and participate in the carnage!

Sharing Silver Coins[edit]

At the end of each war, you may earn silver coins which you can share with other members of your order. You can share a coin with the same order member once per day, beginning at 6 AM (ET). The amount of silver coins you can share (displayed as "Times Sendable") will no longer be reset every war.

Schedule[edit]

Warring Period (ET): 10 PM May 15th, 2013 - 12 AM May 20th, 2013

Schedule of a War Term[edit]

  • 00:00 START
  • 45 min. DECLARATION OF WAR
  • 00:45 (end of declaration of war/start of matching)
  • 15 min. MATCHING
  • 01:00 (end of matching/start of war)
  • 60 min WAR
  • 02:00 FINISH


War Term Start Times[edit]

Holy Wars take place from May 15th, 2013 to May 20th, 2013 (ET). On those days, war terms start every hour on the hour all day.

Holy War Schedule
12 AM 1 AM 2 AM
3 AM 4 AM 5 AM
6 AM 7 AM 8 AM
9 AM 10 AM 11 AM
12 PM 1 PM 2 PM
3 PM 4 PM 5 PM
6 PM 7 PM 8 PM
9 PM 10 PM 11 PM
  • The last term begins at 9 PM (ET). The war period ends at 11 PM (ET), with results visible until 12 AM (ET).

Caution

  • The first 45 minutes of every war term are the war declaration period, when orders can declare war. To take part in a war, declare war during this period. Any member of an order can do so from the main Holy Wars screen.
  • The next 15 minutes are the matching period, when an opposing order is automatically selected.
  • The final 60 minutes of the war period are the war, when the matched-up orders fight.
  • War cannot be declared during the matching period or the actual war.
  • An order can't be involved in more than one war at the same time. That means that an order fighting in the two-hour term that starts 7:00 PM can't declare war again at 8:00 PM. The soonest they can declare war is at 9:00 PM.

Victory Rewards[edit]

An order gets 1 victory point every time it wins a war. When Holy Wars ends, orders receive rewards according to how many victory points they collected. These victory point rewards can only be collected by users who meet the individual holy war point requirements.

Victory Points: 16+
Rewards:

  • Holy Wars XIII: Helter Skelter HW SR Exchange Ticket x 2
  • Bandit King (High Rare) x 2
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 4

Only order members with at least 30,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 16+ victory points. Members with 5,000 - 29,999 points will get the reward for 12-15 victory points. Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 8-11 victory points.


Victory Points: 12-15
Rewards:

  • Holy Wars XIII: Helter Skelter HW SR Exchange Ticket x 1
  • Bandit King (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 2

Only order members with at least 5,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 12-15 victory points. Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 8-11 victory points.


Victory Points: 8-11
Rewards:

  • Bandit King (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 1

Only order members with at least 1,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 8-11 victory points.
