Notice: RoB Servers have finally shut down due to the incompetence of the owning company. It was fun for me working on the wiki with all of you and I do hope I have been a somewhat competent admin. Let's meet again in a better world! ;)


Edit: One last note for those of you who are still reading the wiki and want to save their favourite cards: Ezo (the owner of the wiki) said he will keep the wiki running for a little while after the servers close down. So feel free to save whatever you like - or use the Japanese RoB wiki since the game will still be running in Japan. --Morgan (talk) 07:09, 29 February 2016 (EST)'

Notice: Since I was asked about this: Use this Link to get to the Japanese ROB Wiki. For those of you who can't read Japanese: on the left side of the screen use the 3rd row of coloured links that reads 画像一覧. Those coloured links will guide you to the card overview of the specific realm. Here you will find nearly every card shown in it's final evolution. Click on any card you like to open up the card page. To get a high res pic of the card, click the link right below the picture of the evolution state you wanna see. Keep in mind: the stats of the cards differ from our version and West ROB exclusive cards will not be shown. --Morgan (talk) 13:36, 9 March 2016 (EST)

Notice: Anyone wishing to play on the Japanese ROB needs to sign up on the Japanese Mobage website. For those who have done so, you need to go to the ROB webpage to start playing. Also, thanks for making my stay here so nice. I hope to see you guys in the Shadowverse beta and ultimately the game itself. How about you guys consider migrating to the Japanese version after playing on the English servers this time, in case they shut down? --NekoDesu (talk) 21:07, 14 March 2016 (EDT)
