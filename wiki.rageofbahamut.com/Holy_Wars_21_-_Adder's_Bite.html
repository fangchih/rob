Changes from Past Holy Wars[edit]

Legend Reward Cards
Legend cards are now a part of the ranking rewards.
Improved Ranking Rewards
Other ranking rewards are better than ever.
Upgraded Mission Rewards
HR reward cards and SR exchange tickets are easier to get. There are also a few more mission rewards than before.
New Herald Skills
Heralds now have three additional skills.
  • Plans may change without notice. Thank you for understanding.

New Herald Skills[edit]

Barricade Serenade
Effect:
This gives all order members a boost to DEF for a short period of time.
Cooldown:
6 minutes from launch.
Battle March
  • Available from position LVL 2.
Effect:
This gives all order members a boost to ATK for a short period of time.
Cooldown:
6 minutes from launch.
Rising Rondo
  • Available from position LVL 3.
Effect:
This gives all order members a boost to holy war points gained for a short period of time.
Cooldown:
6 minutes from launch.
  • Note:
Up to three of these skills can be used at one time by your order.

Unrestricted Cards[edit]

Rewards

Card

Notes
  • You can reset the box once you draw 1 card of SSR rariy.
If you refill the box, the box returns to it's original state. There is no way to undo a refill, so please be careful. You can refill up to 3 times.
  • Cards from holy war card packs will be sent to your Presents (Device Registration Required). You will be able to collect your cards once you have verified your device.
Even when there are no cards left in the box, the box will not automatically refill. in that case, if you get a Gold Coin CP you will get one of the 'Unrestricted Cards'.
The event may end without prior notice. Once the holy war card pack period ends, your silver coins and gold coins will disappear.
