Story[edit]

Your bleary eyes begin to focus on the carvings of the temple ceiling above you. Temple? How did you get here? If only there were time to unravel the mystery...

Rumor says your old ally Aether is about to rush you; she's sure you've led a mutiny. Though you'd like to run, your wan-faced shipmate Lecia is kneeling in agony before a willowy girl of mystery.

There's only one way out of this predicament. Find it in The Three Mutineers!

How to Play[edit]

Gather echoes to make echo seals Your order will get a big bonus to lost memories! Battle lots of raid bosses to up the bonus!

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Lotus seeds, aqua candies, and sumac candies are available for free on the event screen once every day

Bonus High Rares Just for Playing![edit]

Visit the main event screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available when you proceed through the event; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 utopian powders (personal), which let you hit raid bosses with 16 times the damage of a 1-AP attack! Claim them from your Presents after the event starts.

You can change the utopian powders (personal) into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.

If the event preview gets enough likes and shares on facebook you'll get even more utopian powders (personal) when the event starts.
