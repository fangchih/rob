Changes from last time[edit]

Berries
Berries can be used to fully recover user's ATK PWR and DEF PWR.
Unused berries are carried over to the next match, but not to the next event.
*Berries cannot be used for Blitz Attacks.
Event Rewards
Some event rewards have been changed.
Tiers
Users/orders are placed in one of five tiers: platinum, gold, silver, bronze or copper.
A user's/order's tier is determined by their rank in previous Onslaughts, with more weight given to more recent Onslaughts.
