Time Period: 9 PM June 6th, 2013 to 9 PM June 9th, 2013

Advance to later treasure grids to win great prizes! Win Legend event cards that give you the upper hand in Memories of the Dark!


Card Pack Details[edit]

There are seven grid levels, with the middle grid of each level being one of the following cards: SR Veight, SSR Helsing or Legend Aphrodite. There are two version of this CP: a 4-card pack and a 11-card pack. You are guaranteed to obtain rewards from 1 to 10 squares depending on the CP you buy, and you are guaranteed to get every last square reward when you purchase enough packs. The first 4-card pack of each level is discounted.

Each level also increases your chances of earning Helsing or Aphrodite on the middle square:

Level 2 Helsing drop rate + 10%
Level 3 Helsing drop rate + 15%
Level 4 Helsing drop rate + 25%
Level 5 Helsing always drops!
Level 6 Aphrodite drop rate doubles!
Level 7 Aphrodite always drops!


Note: After you get every last square on the level 7 grid, you can return to level 1. The Treasure Grid CP becomes unavailable once you return to level 1 four times and get all the square rewards on level 7.
