About Rewards[edit]

Ranking rewards will be distributed once calculations and preparations have been made after the event is over. We will inform you once they have been distributed. All rewards of the event will be sent to 'Presents that need verification.' You cannot claim your reward if you have not completed device registration.

Order Victory Reward[edit]

You can earn rewards depending on the number of Holy Wars your Order wins. So cooperate together with your Order members to get the most wins and the most rewards!

  • Gold Rewards Order Victories: 45+ Reward: Dark Angel Olivia (S Rare) x 2 Shield Angel (High Rare) x 1
    • Only Order members with at least 30,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Gold Rewards. Order members with 5,000-29,999 pts will receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 pts will receive Bronze Rewards.
  • Silver Rewards Order Victories: 25-44 Reward: Dark Angel Olivia (S Rare) x 1 Shield Angel (High Rare) x 1
    • Only Order members with at least 5,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 pts will receive Bronze Rewards.
  • Bronze Rewards Order Victories: 15-24 Reward: Shield Angel (High Rare) x 1
    • Only Order members with at least 1,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Bronze Rewards.
    • Order members with fewer than 1,000 Holy War Points will not receive this reward, regardless of their Order's number of Holy War victories.
    • Order Victories refer to an Order's number of Holy Wars won, not an individual user's number of battles won.
