Story[edit]

The tale that began in Time Chronicles continues


After two hundred cycles, armageddon was at last

averted, at the cost of Monika's life. Now a

misunderstanding brings the party back in time

through the use of Pasha's strange powers. Why has

Lucifer chosen the path of the fallen angel? Unlocking

this mystery may be the key to delivering the world

from the coming End.


How to Play[edit]

Defeat bosses to get items! Use Items to train the Wizard!

Some items enhance ATK, others DEF!

The more you use, the better he gets!

Cards awarded after the event!


Rank on top of your grouping every day

for a legend CP Claim Ticket!

New Features[edit]

3rd Birthday Quest
The 3rd Birthday quest contains a branching story that spans multiple eras of time.
As you progress through the quest, choices will appear that will enable you to take part in multiple quests simultaneously.
The destination you select will determine what story unfolds, as well as how the story ends.
The Cycles
View The Cycles to see locked quests within multiple other quests or the unlocked quests for each time period.
3rd Birthday reward
A Stone Tablet will be added to the final ranking reward.



Wizard[edit]

  • Train Wizard using the tomes and spheres that raid bosses drop when defeated.
  • Users who train the Wizard a lot will get a special Legend card at the end of the event. Users who didn't will still get a significantly weaker SS Rare card, but it won't be as good. Wizards's exact stats, alignment, and evolution depend on the type and number of items used in training.


Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours.

  • One Elixir recovers 1 AP during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the Dragoon's Dilemma page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.
