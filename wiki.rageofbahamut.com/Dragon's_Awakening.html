Story[edit]

In the keep
of the Castle in the Sky
is a chamber long hidden
from human eyes.

At last you open
its heavy wood doors.
Therein rests a blue dragon.
Therein wakes a vampiress.

When calamity befalls
the cursed Castle in the Sky,
the dragon will wake and take flight.

How to Play This Event[edit]

Defeat the approaching raid bosses with a ten-card deck! By defeating the required raid bosses, you can open the Portal to Unreality, from which hidden boss may emerge! Cooperate with your friends to defeat the boss and get rewards!

Feed Your Dragon[edit]

Feed cards and berries to your dragon. You'll raise her weight LVL and get rewards!

  • The dragon only eats certain cards. Here's what you can feed her:
    • High Rare cards
    • High Rare cards fully evolved to S Rare
    • Rare cards
    • Rare cards fully evolved to High Rare
    • Some cards from the event quest


Event Participation Bonus[edit]

Every day of the Dragon's Awakening event, when you go to the event screen Vania gives you ice cream scoops and pink dragon jewels.

  • Ice cream scoops recover AP, which you need to attack raid bosses.
  • Pink dragon jewels are exchangeable for rewards.
  • The event participation bonus is reset daily at 6 AM (ET).
