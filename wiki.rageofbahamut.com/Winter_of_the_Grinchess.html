Story[edit]

Winter has arrived. Snow falls, fluttering to and fro upon chilly winds. A night of celebration approaches...

The hero appears as preparations are underway. Holiday cheer invigorates the town and its folk.

Little do they know, that a force works in the shadows, waiting to put their hapiness on ice...

How to Play[edit]

Play through the event quest Cooperate to defeat raid bosses! Win exclusive event cards!

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Holy candies and shiny crests are available for free on the event screen once every day.

  • Each holy candy recovers one AP used to attack raid bosses.
  • You can exchange shiny crests for rewards by tapping Gift Crests on the Rewards tab of the event screen.
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed one every 24 hours, starting from 5 AM (ET).

Free Card[edit]

Visit the main event screen during Winter of the Grinchess to get a free High Rare event cards. Three more copies are available in the quest. Evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 Zenos Powders, which you can use to deal twice the damage of a blitz attack against raid bosses! Claim them from your Presents after the event starts.

Zenos Powder will change into holy powder (personal) after the Winter of the Grinchess event ends.

Notes[edit]

  • Any user can declare.
  • You don't need to declare to be able to participate in this event.
  • You can pick up your declaration items by visiting the main event screen after the event starts!
