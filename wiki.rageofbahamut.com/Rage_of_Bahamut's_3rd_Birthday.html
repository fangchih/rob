Promotion 4 - Welcome Back Promotion[edit]

Bring a Friend back to the game, get great rewards!

Friends brought back Reward
3 Hell's Unleasher x 1
2 Elspree Sawmaiden x 1
1 Elspree Sawmaiden x 1
How to Welcome Back
  • Users can only enter a welcome back code if they have not played since 9 PM, Jan. 20th, 2015 (ET)
  • A welcome back code can be used up to three times. It will become invalid after that.
  • Once a friend enters your welcome back code, a reward will be sent directly to your presents.
  • The welcome back code is valid until 9 PM Mar. 27th, 2015 (ET). It will become invalid after that.

Promotion 5 - Daily Birthday Play Bonus[edit]

Play every day to get rewards!

Play a total of 30 days during the promotion to get a special 3rd Birthday S Rare card!
