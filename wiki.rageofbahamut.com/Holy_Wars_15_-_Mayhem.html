Changes from Past Holy Wars[edit]

Exploring
You'll be able to get statues of wisdom, which can boost the number of holy war points you get.
Statue of Wisdom - This gives all members of your order a boost to the holy war points that they achieve when they win battles for a certain amount of time. While one is active, getting a second and third one will increase the boost. Each one after that will make the boost last longer.


Holy War Schedule
You're now able to cancel or change war declarations and fully customize your Holy Wars schedule! View the new schedule and participate in the carnage!


Holy War Coins
The holy wars mystery box has been totally revamped! Plus, Lucius the Mercenary (S Rare) will be waiting in the mystery box!

Victory Rewards[edit]

An order gets 1 victory point every time it wins a war. When Holy Wars ends, orders receive rewards according to how many victory points they collected. These victory point rewards can only be collected by users who meet the individual holy war point requirements.

Victory Points: 20
Rewards:

  • [Holy Wars XIV: Mayhem] SSR Exchange Ticket x 1

Only order members with at least 200,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 20 victory points.
Members with 30,000 - 199,999 points will get the reward for 16-19 victory points.
Members with 5,000 - 29,999 points will get the reward for 12-15 victory points.
Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 8-11 victory points.


Victory Points: 16-19
Rewards:

  • Wild Bandit (High Rare) x 2
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 4
  • [Holy Wars XIV: Mayhem] SR Exchange Ticket x 2

Only order members with at least 30,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 16-19 victory points.
Members with 5,000 - 29,999 points will get the reward for 12-15 victory points.
Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 8-11 victory points.


Victory Points: 12-15
Rewards:

  • Wild Bandit (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 2
  • [Holy Wars XIV: Mayhem] SR Exchange Ticket x 1

Only order members with at least 5,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 12-15 victory points.
Members with 1,000 - 4,999 points will get the reward for 8-11 victory points.


Victory Points: 8-11
Rewards:

  • Wild Bandit (High Rare) x 1
  • Premium CP Claim Ticket x 1

Only order members with at least 1,000 individual holy war points can get the reward for 8-11 victory points.
