About Rookie Battle Ranking[edit]

Time Limit: 3 days from when you first start the game[edit]

Rookie Battle Ranking ranks users based on Honor Points achieved through battles against others during a 24-hour period (starting at 5 AM ET). After your 3 days in the Rookie Battle Ranking, you will be transferred to the regular Battle Ranking.

About Honor Points[edit]

You earn Honor Points by winning battles against other users. You get additional bonus Honor Points by beating High Rankers.

About High Rankers[edit]

High Rankers are the top 1000 users in Battle Ranking. The High Ranker list is reset daily when rankings are calculated at 5:00 AM (ET).

About Titles and Rewards[edit]

You can earn Titles and rewards by meeting certain conditions. You can go to the rewards page from 'Top' or 'My Page' during the week after Rookie Battle Ranking ends. Once the period ends, you can claim your rewards at any time via a link on 'Your Previous Battles.' (tap your username at the top of 'My Page')

About 'Counterstrike' points[edit]

If another user picks a battle with you and you successfully defeat them, you will earn 'Counterstrike' pts added to your regular Honor pts.

Battle Awards[edit]

The Rookie Battle Ranking Battle Award ranking is based on total Honor Points received over 3 days. Make it to the top of the ranking and you can get rewards, such as cards! Once rankings have been calculated on the last day, Battle Awards will be sent to your Presents. Claim rewards from your Presents. You can see the results of Rookie Battle Ranking for only 1 week after it ends, via 'Top' or 'My Page.' Should you not make the rankings, you will still get 1000 Friendship Points from participation.
