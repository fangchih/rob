Story[edit]

Solar jewelry had long brought the blessings of the sun to the land of Solheim.

WHen a rival kingdoms royalty plundered that jewelry, Solheim was swallowed up by unrelenting rain and darkness. The sun-starved crops began to wither, and Solheim's fate seemed uncertain...

You vow to help Pascale, the Oracle of the Sun, recover the jewelry... even if it means going up against the rival king and the god who watches over him

How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade! Fight bosses and compete to collect the most tangerine gems! Collect jewels and exchange them for great rewards!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect the most tangerine gems during the campaign.
  • This event will have nine daily 19-hour campaigns.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 8 PM Jan. 15th, 2014 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Thermal salves and solar rings are available for free on the event screen once every day.

  • One thermal salve recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Solar rings can be exchanged for rwards. On the Tempestuous Reign screen, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewelry button.

Bonus High Rares Just for Playing![edit]

Visit the main Tempestuous Reign screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available in the quest; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 scaldrocks, which you can use to perfectly dry all enemies when fighting storm fiends. In battles against Regnlund troopers, scaldrocks boost ATK by 20%

scaldrocks will change into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.
