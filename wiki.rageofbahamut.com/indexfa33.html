Description[edit]

In Rage of Bahamut a player can acquire several kinds of items. Some of them can be earned by just playing the game, whereas other items must be bought in the Shop. The Items in the game are subject to continuous change. Prior to the Ice Throne event, a new type of event item was introduces with every event. After Ice Throne all future event items were replaced by Ultimate Powder. On June 20th, 2012 personal forms of Holy Powder and Cure Water were introduced. Later Mini Holy Powder and Mini Cure Water were added to the game.

The following list gives an overview of the existing items:

Standard Items[edit]

Friendship Points[edit]

Usage
Can be used to buy Friendship Card Packs
Obtained from
for all the different ways of earning Friendship points, please refer to the Friendship Points page.


Holy Powder[edit]

Usage
  • Fully recovers ATK PWR and DEF PWR.
Obtain from
  • Defeating a boss
  • Shop costs 100 Rage Medals
  • Login Bonus (in form of a Reward CP)

Cure Water[edit]

Usage
  • Fully recovers stamina.
Obtain from
  • Defeating a boss
  • Shop costs 100 Rage Medals

Magic Circle[edit]

Usage
  • Protects 1 treasure from being targeted once by defeating the opponent upon start of battle.
Obtain from
  • Defeating a boss
  • Shop costs 100 Rage Medals
  • Exchange 20 Gems

Holy Powder (Personal)[edit]

Usage
  • Fully recovers ATK PWR and DEF PWR. Cannot be traded.
Obtain from
  • Defeating a boss
  • 3 Million+ Users Campaign
  • Login Bonus
  • Event Rewards
  • Exchange 20 Gems

Tis Item cannot be traded.

Cure Water (Personal)[edit]

Usage
  • Fully recovers stamina. Cannot be traded.
Obtain from
  • Defeating a boss
  • 3 Million+ Users Campaign
  • Login Bonus
  • Event Rewards
  • Exchange 20 Gems

Tis Item cannot be traded.

Mini Holy Powder/ Cure Water[edit]

Usage
  • Half the effect of a normal Holy Powder/Cure Water
Obtain from
  • Event Rewards

Tis Item cannot be traded.

Ultimate Powder/Personal Ultimate Powder[edit]

Usage
  • effective in all raid boss events
  • deals 16 times the damage of a 1-AP attack
  • can be changed to Holy Powder/Holy Powder (Personal) at any time
  • can be carried over to the next raid boss event
Obtain from
  • Personal Ultimate Powder can be obtained from event rewards
  • Ultimate Powder can only be bought from the Shop or with Card Packs

Alchemic Philter[edit]

Usage
  • upon usage this item resets all attributes to 20. Additional points will be returned to the boost pool.
Obtain from
  • can only be bought at the Shop for 500 RageMedals

Human Stone[edit]

Usage
  • upon usage this item changes your realm to "Man".
Obtain from
  • can only be bought at the Shop for 500 RageMedals

Divine Stone[edit]

Usage
  • upon usage this item changes your realm to "Gods".
Obtain from
  • can only be bought at the Shop for 500 RageMedals

Demonic Stone[edit]

Usage
  • upon usage this item changes your realm to "Demon".
Obtain from
  • can only be bought at the Shop for 500 RageMedals

Pet Dragon Items[edit]

All of these items can be purchased from the Merchant on your Pet Dragon's page.
The first elemental stone is free.
Each elemental stone can only be purchased once every three days.

Fang Berry[edit]

Can only be bought once every 24 hours

Usage
  • Raise your dragon's EXP by 500.
First Purchase Price
  • 1000 Rupies
Later purchases
  • Second purchase is 10,000, and all subsequent purchases cost 20,000.
Description
  • Dragons' favorite berries. Once in a blue moon, they fall from the sky.

Ancient Soul[edit]

Usage
  • Raise your dragon's LVL by 5.
Price
  • 100 MobaCoin
Description
  • The soul of an ancient royal dragon which has perished. These supposedly serve as an excellent power source for living dragons.

Embryonic Shard[edit]

Usage
  • Resets your dragon to its most basic form, setting it to level 1 and erasing its Uninherited skills but keeping the skill points. One Uninherited Skill is kept by your "new" dragon as an Inherited Skill (up to six Inherited Skills.)
  • Cannot carry more than one.
First Purchase Price
  • 50,000 Rupies
Increment
25,000
Description
  • A mysterious stone which contains the unmodified genetics of a baby dragon.

Usage

  • Can only be used a max level baby dragon
First Purchase Price
