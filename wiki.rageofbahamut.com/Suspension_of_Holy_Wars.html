Suspension of Holy Wars

From Rage of Bahamut WIKI
Jump to: navigation, search

Changes made[edit]

  • Holy Wars are suspended and will now longer be supported (by mobage)
  • existing Holy Wars skills will be changed to Castle Crusher Skills
  • There's a possibility, that maybe Holy Wars will return in a distant future


Original Rage of Bahamut News[edit]

In order to free up development resources and raise the

overall quality of Rage, we have decided to indefinitely

suspend Holy Wars. To reduce setbacks to participants,

all Holy Wars skills will be converted to Castle Crushers

skills for the time being. If Holy Wars ever returns with

similar rules, those skills will revert.


We would like to apologize for the suddenness of this

change, which we know will likely disappointment some

Holy Wars fans. For now, please give Castle Crushers a

try while we work to give you a better game than ever.


Rage of Bahamut Team
