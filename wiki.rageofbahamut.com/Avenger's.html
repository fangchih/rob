Point Exchange Details[edit]

  • Each time you exchange your points, you'll unlock greater rewards for that reward type.
  • You can only get each reward once
  • Points will disappear after they are exchanged
  • After the card pack time period has ended, you'll still be able to exchange your leftover points during the point exchange period.

Other Details[edit]

  • The card pack animation may be skipped depending on a user's connection.
  • If the loot that you get in the flash differs from what is shown on the results screen, the loot displayed on the on the results screen is correct
  • Even if the card pack animation is skipped, you'll still get the loot that you purchased. Check your Presents.

Notes[edit]

  • You get one point for each Hope card pack you purchase at the 300 price point.
  • You get 10-12 points for each Hope card pack you purchase at the 2,700 price point.
  • After you accumulate 641 points, you will no longer be able to purchase the Hope card pack at the 2,700 price point.
  • You will no longer be able to purchase the card pack after you accumulate 650 points.
