Changes Holy Wars[edit]

Holy War Cards
Holy War cards will not have special skills
for this Onslaught. However, there are
plans in place for major updates, including
new skills for Holy War cards.
Blitz Attack
Renamed from Full Force Attack
Support Deck
A five-card support deck has been added. A
portion of the ATK and DEF of the support
deck will be added to the main deck.
Notes:
  • Support decks come into play only when the main
ATK/DEF deck used is 100 PWR or more.
  • The ATK/DEF bonus from support decks is not
factored into the real ATK/DEF numbers. It will
apply during Onslaught battles.
  • Support decks do not use ATK/DEF PWR.
Ultimate Shingeki
The oder leader can use a new skill called
Ultimate Shingeki. This skill can only be
used once per day.
Rush Bonus
Rush bonuses have been added. Reach the
point threshold before a rival order to get a
boost to onslaught points.
In each battle, the first order to score a preset number
of points will get a rush bonus. This bonus lets members
score 20% more points for 10 minutes.
Notes:
  • The preset number of points is based on the two
order's performance in the battle right before.
  • When both orders score the preset number of points
at the same time, both get the rush bonus.
Dragon skills
Dragon skills cannot be used in this
Onslaught. They will be enabled in future
Onslaughts.
Missions
New Missions for killer completions and
badges have been added.
Coin Exchange
There are two different boxes with varying
content.
Gold coins and silver coins can be used to draw
rewards from boxes.
Earning coins:
Every time a user scores 100,000 onslaught points in
one battle, three gold coins are added to their total. Up
to fifteen gold coins can be added to their total in a
single battle.
Every time a user scores 3,000 onslaught points in one
battle, one silver coin is added to their total. Additional
silver coins are added for high scores and high in-order
ranks.
Boxes:
There are two boxes, the coin box and the supreme
coin box. It takes one gold coin or three silver coins to
draw from the coin box. It takes two gold coins to
draw from the supreme coin box.
Notes:
  • A user can earn up to 300 coins in a single battle,
but not more.
Orestones
30 silver orestones appear for every personal holy
powder you use in a blitz attack. 30 silver orestones
and one gold orestone appear for each tradable HP
you use in a blitz attack. Both gold orestones and
silver orestones could appear when you're exploring.
Ten silver orestones can be converted into one silver
coin. One gold orestone can be converted into one gold
coin.
Notes:
  • Orestones disappear after the end of the event. They
don't carry over to the next Onslaught.
Role Skill Effects
Some role skill effects have been changed.

New Skill effects[edit]

Skills or positions not listed, remain unchanged.


Order Leader
Shingeki

Duration: 5 Minutes
Cost: 1,000 SP
Vice Leader

ATK Leader
Warcry
Hit to enemies DEF reduced.

DEF Leader
