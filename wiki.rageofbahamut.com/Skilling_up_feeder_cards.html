Skilling up Cards and Feeders[edit]

The charts below use the base skill charts that can be found at the Skill boost probability page. There are, of course, several different ways to achieve much of the skilling using lower rarity cards with higher skills. In this case, a cap of S Rare feeders was used for enhancement skilling X Legends and a cap of High Rare feeders was used for skilling everything else, except where skilling from 9 to 10 in X Legend and Legend in which two cards of the next higher rarity were used to bring the total from 88 to 100.5%. The cards were chosen to avoid skilling the feeders as much as possible and stay mostly under the rarity caps set, and to also achieve 100% or better on the skill up probability. At the higher skill levels, 7+ or so, of Legend and SSRare, and most of X Legend, it would probably be more cost effective to use a pair of Devil Queens to achieve 100% rather than skilling feeders to higher levels.

Base values can be calculated using the skill-level probability formula. The percentages were calculated using those base values and this formula: 


Percent = Base + ((Feeder Skill Level - 1) * Base / 2)
