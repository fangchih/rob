Differences from the last Holy Wars[edit]

The following changes are planned for this Holy Wars event:

(Please note: plans may change without notice. Thank you for understanding.)

  • New 'Ad Hoc Orders' form automatically with users who aren't in Orders yet, limited to Holy War event periods.
  • During Holy Wars event preparation periods, if you log in at least once a day, you can get 1 Holy Powder (Personal).
    • Evanescent Holy Powder from previous Holy Wars won't be given out.
  • During Holy War events, you can't do the following, so make sure you prepare beforehand:
    • Join an Order
    • Leave an Order
    • Request to join an Order
    • Cancel your request
    • Approve/deny Order join requests (Order Leader only)
    • Create an Order
    • Disband an Order (Order Leader Only)
    • Kick members out (Order Leader only)
    • Appoint a new Order Leader (Order Leader Only)
    • Nominate yourself to be Order Leader
    • Take over as Order Leader
    • Change Roles

Changes That Apply Even Outside Holy Wars

  • Order Leaders get a 5% boost to ATK/DEF during battles.
  • Vice-Leaders get a 3% boost to ATK/DEF during battles.
  • Order buildings have a Building Level. See Orders for more info.
    • The effects above apply to regular battles, not just Holy War battles.

(Please note: plans may change without notice. Thank you for understanding.)

Ad Hoc Orders[edit]

  • If you don't belong to an Order by the time the Holy Wars event starts, you'll be added to an Ad Hoc Order automatically. (This is limited to users who meet the requirements to join Ad Hoc Orders.)

What are Ad Hoc Orders?

  • Ad Hoc Orders are formed automatically during Holy Wars event periods.
  • These are formed with users who aren't in Orders.
  • Users under LVL 20 can't join Ad Hoc Orders.
  • Only users who registered their devices can join Ad Hoc Orders.
  • Even if you are invited to a regular Order, an Ad Hoc Order will form first.
  • Once a Holy War event starts, you won't be able to invite others to your Order.
  • If you have an Order you want to join, make sure to do so before Holy Wars starts!
  • Ad Hoc Orders disband when Holy Wars events end.
  • In Ad Hoc Orders there are no Order Leaders or Vice-Leaders. Leaders are assigned automatically to users in the order that they join the Order.
    • 1: ATK Leader
    • 2: DEF Leader
    • 3 onward: Regular Member (not a Leader)
  • Each Ad Hoc Order has a maximum of 25 members.

In Ad Hoc Orders, You Can:

  • Declare war
  • Donate
  • Build Walls
  • Rupies donated or Walls built do not carry over into other Orders.
  • Ad Hoc Order names are created automatically and can't be changed.
  • Please understand that Ad Hoc Order specs may change without prior notice.

You can earn rewards depending on the number of Holy Wars your Order wins. So cooperate together with your Order members to get the most wins and the most rewards!

  • Order Victories: 50+
  • Reward: Dark Angel Olivia (S Rare) x 2
  • Demonic Knight (High Rare) x 1

Only Order members with at least 30,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Gold Rewards. Order members with 5,000-29,999 Points will receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 Points will receive Bronze Rewards.

  • Order Victories: 30-49
  • Reward: Dark Angel Olivia (S Rare) x 1

Only Order members with at least 5,000 Individual Holy War Points can receive Gold Rewards. Order members with 5,000-29,999 Points will receive Silver Rewards. Order members with 1,000-4,999 Points will receive Bronze Rewards.
