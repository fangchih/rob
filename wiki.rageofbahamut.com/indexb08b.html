RoB shutting down[edit]

As noted today in an ingame message, DeNA has successfully completed killing off Rage of Bahamut and has started the process of shutting down the servers.


If you are displeased with their "efforts to keep the app going" please feel free to leave a comment on their Facebook page [[1]], that - in good old DeNA tradition - will be completely ignored.


Status of the Wiki[edit]

Since DeNA/Mobage killed of the events a lot of people quit with RoB and quit working on the wiki. A few new people started working as well. Thank all of you for your hard work and for the time you spend on the RoB wiki. As of now I think the Wiki will stay online at least until the RoB servers will shut down. Events and card packs will still be added, although I personally don't think it will make much of a difference. After the game servers will be shut down, the owner of the wiki will have to decide if he leaves the page on his server or if he will delete it with all it's data.
