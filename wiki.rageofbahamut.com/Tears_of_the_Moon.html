Synopsis[edit]

There will be limited-time quests to get rare cards by completing treasure series. Furthermore you will be able to slay daunting enemies waiting in the Legendary Tower. Rewards will be handed out by ranking so keep progressing through the quests and aim for the top.

Info About This Event[edit]

Every few centuries, the moon sheds some tears. Those are a sign that the moon is approaching. Bent on stealing the moon's magical powers, evil emissaries from hell have started to head to the Legendary Tower. You must chase them up the tower and stop them from using the moon's magic to take over the world.

Tears of the Moon[edit]

In this past event, at random you would intitate the quest, a special cut scene will trigger, granting you the "Tears of the Moon" buff. When this buff is active, you would use less stamina for questing, allowing you to proceed longer without the need to recharge.

  • Stage 1 : 20% Reduction
  • Stage 2 : 30% Reduction
  • Stage 3 : 50% Reduction & Random Award

Each stage lasted 5 Minutes, and upon activating another stage, the time limit would restart at 5 minutes. You would only get to stage 2 from stage 1 and to stage 3 from stage 2.

Triggering Stage 3 would grant you a chance to obtain any of the awards by random.

Additional Rewards[edit]

Gold Fox is rewarded for defeating the Boss in Event Quest 50 and 100.

If you finished Event Quest 100 and collected all 6 Treasure Series you will recieve Master Pop Star x1 as reward.

When you reach 3rd stage in the Tears of the Moon effect, you will retrieve one of the following rewards:

  • 1 personal Holy Powder
  • 10 personal Holy Powder
  • 1 personal Cure Water
  • 10 personal Cure Water
  • 50000 rupies
  • 10000 rupies
  • Devil Queen
  • Angel Queen
  • Gold Dragon
  • Hekate
  • Nothing (if the 3rd stage animation has been skipped)

Event Treasures[edit]

  • Moon Harp | Reward: Astolfo
  • Moon Cradle | Reward: Lampetia
  • Moon Poker | Reward: Werewolf
  • Moon Harp+ | Reward: Astolfo+
  • Moon Cradle+ | Reward: Lampetia+
  • Moon Poker+ | Reward: Werewolf+

The event+ versions only appear when you have collected the normal versions and have completed the first 50 quests.

Additionally, the event+ reward cards' ATK and DEF do not include the 5% carry-over that would normally be included when two base cards are evolved. For example, Astolfo has an ATK of 2290. When evolving two unenhanced Astolfos, the resulting ATK of Astolfo+ would be (2290 * 1.2 + 2290 * 0.05 + 2290 * 0.05) = 2978, rounding up each of the 5% carry-overs. But the Astolfo+ rewarded for completing the Harp+ treasure set actually has an ATK of 2748 which is exactly 2290 * 1.2.



Glitch: Tear-fishing[edit]

A glitch in which Tears will fall even if you do not use any stamina. Commonly used as of time written as a way to quickly lvl, climb the tower, or getting the 3rd tear rewards.

Status: Resolved
