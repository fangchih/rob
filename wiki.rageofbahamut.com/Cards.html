General Information[edit]

Rage of Bahamut has over 600 different cards which is constantly updated.
Please go to the cardlist to see all the cards and their stats.

Card Parameters[edit]

Every card has the following parameters:

Parameter Name Description
Alignment A card's Alignment shows its allegiance to a specific realm (Man,God, or Demon), and affects a card's
chance to activate Skills in battle, and whether or not they receive boosts from Order Buildings and Realms.

Rarity
A card's Rarity is a indicator on how powerful a card is. There are currently seven levels of rarity:
Normal > High Normal > Rare > High Rare > S Rare > SS Rare > Legend > S Legend

Card Level
A card's Level is how strong a card is, and the higher the level of a card the more powerful it will be.
All cards have a level cap, depending on their Rarity:

Normal: 20 levels
High Normal: 30 levels
Rare: 40 levels
High Rare: 50 levels
S Rare: 60 levels
SS Rare: 70 levels
Legend: 80 levels
S Legend: 90(?) levels
Evolution Stage
A card's Evolution Stage is how far along a card is during Evolution. There are four Stages:

Stage 1 Cards: These cards are the Base forms of cards.

Stage 2 Cards: These cards are recognized by a plus (+) sign at the end of their name (Cardname+).

Stage 3 Cards: These cards are recognized by two plus (+) signs at the end of their name (Cardname++).

Stage 4 Cards: These are the last Stage of cards, and are noted by having a
bracketed title in the front of the name ( [Title] Cardname), and given an increased Rarity.

PWR
Amount of PWR required to place a card within a deck. The higher the power,
the more Attack Power and Defense Power a player will need in order to use them.

ATK The ATK of a card shows how much damage it will do when either attacking an opposing player or a boss.
DEF The DEF if a card shows how much damage it will do when being attacked by an opposing player.
Skill Some cards have skills which have different effects as describe, and can change the outcome of a battle.
