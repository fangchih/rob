Story[edit]

Someone has created a town inhabited solely by

puppets. But who has been turning people into these

clockwork creations and amassing them into an

unliving army? And who is the puppet who bears an

uncanny resemblance to Orchis?


Go now, forge your own way...


How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade!

Banish wanderers to get items!

Writhing Horror yield the most!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more panchrestia drops than other brigades.
  • Emerald globes drop when you comfort spirits.
  • This event will have eight daily 19-hour campaigns. The number of emerald globes that brigades collect is calulated per campaign.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 8 PM Jan. 3rd, 2015 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

New Features[edit]

New Enemies
The writhing horror is a new type of enemy that appears from Hollow Spire, Upper Reach, Level 1.
Hollow Spire, Secret Sanctum Shard Bonus
The more wanderers that appear in the hollow spite, secret sanctum, the more ranking shards will drop.
Banish Chain Bonus
Extra ranking shards drop when you banish multiple groups of wanderers in a row.
Rebalanced Rewards
The special rewards have been rebalanced.
Questing Item Tickets
Questing item tickets are a new reward that can be exchanged for event items in the next questing event.
Brigade Bar Added to Quest Screen
The brigade bar is now visible on the Quest screen
Top 3 Users Animation
A new animation displays the top three users from each campaign.
  • The animation can be toggled on and off from the Event Settings screen.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting at the beginning of your campaign.

  • One Elixir recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the Unstrung page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.

Killer Completion Rewards[edit]

To earn a killer completion, you must win a campaign and collect the required number of Ranking Shards during it.

Extra Killer Completions
In the last campaign, your brigade can recieve an extra killer completion if it earns at least 1,5 times more Ranking Shards than the opponent.
All brigades have a chance at the extra killer completion, no matter how many campaigns they win or lose.


Notes:

  • Victory points and killer completions are awarded at the start of the campaign following your brigade's victory.
