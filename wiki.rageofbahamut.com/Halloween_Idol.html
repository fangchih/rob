Story[edit]

Mobs of wild jack o'laterns are terrorizing Skeleton

City. Stop them or the Halloween Idol contest goes belly

up, together with Melvie's dreams of being number one.

The spookiest time of the year is lurking right around

the corner...

How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade!

Disenchant jack o'lanterns to get items!

Great pumpkins yield the most!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more Ranking Shards than other brigades.
  • Emerald globes drop when you comfort spirits.
  • This event will have eight daily 19-hour campaigns. The number of emerald globes that brigades collect is calculated per campaign.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 9 PM Oct. 22nd, 2014 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

New Features[edit]

Rupie Snare
A Rupie Snare button now appears on the Quest screen.
Tap this button to use 5000 Rupies and boost your chance of disenchanting jack o'lanterns for five minutes.
* Rupie snare does not make it any more likely that you perfectly disenchant jack o'lanterns.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting at the beginning of your campaign.

  • One Elixir recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the The Halloween Idol page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 personal disanimate potions, which you can use to perfectly disenchant jack o'lanterns and boost ATK by 20% in battles against trick-or-treaters.

You can change personal disanimate potions into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.

All brigades have a chance at the extra killer completion, no matter how many campaigns they win or lose.
