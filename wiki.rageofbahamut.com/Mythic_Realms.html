About Mythic Realms Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Construct a one-realm powerhouse!


Buy four packs in one realm,

get an S Rare - Legend card!

The card will be from the same realm!

Completion Rewards[edit]

Draw four packs from one realm to get an S

Rare - Legend card!


Draw five packs from every realm to get 50

disanimate potions and an SS Rare - Legend

card. The card will have a single-realm boost!

Notes[edit]

  • There are three kinds of Mythic Realms card packs: Gods packs, Demons packs and Man packs. Each pack contains cards from one realm.
  • Draw four packs of one kind to get an S Rare - Legend card.
  • Draw five packs of all three kinds and get an SS Rare - Legend card which has a single-realm boost.
  • The realm of the SS Rare - Legend card can't be specified.
  • After you receive the SS Rare - Legend card, the Mythic Realm card pack can be reset. The card pack ends once your receive the third SS Rare- Legend card.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
