Time Period: 9 PM May 7th, 2013 to 11 PM May 13th, 2013


About this event[edit]

A new individual event to pass the time and earn some items, Rupies, HP, CW, cards, CP Tickets, and Friendship Points WHILE at the same time earning Honor Points.

You earn Stamps of Excellence which can be exchanged for Holy Powder (Personal) at the end of the event.
Each stamp earns you 1 Holy Powder (Personal).
Earn 18 Stamps and get an additional 3 Holy Powder (Personal) as a bonus.

Battle[edit]

You must battle by clicking the Dastardly Dragon icon/graphic, then clicking on Battle.

If you don't, you will not earn Azurite Crystals nor your Stamps.

How to Reset the Rewards[edit]

When you give azurite crystals back to the dragon, he will in return give you cards and items he keeps in his mystery box. Each box contains a specific number of specific items and cards.

You'll be able to reset the box when you get:

Handful: Devil Queen Sackful: Doubly Dead Demon

Caution

  • Rewards are correctly credited to your account, even when the Returned Crystals screen is not displayed.
  • You cannot receive rewards from an empty mystery box.
