How to draw[edit]

  • First, get the 12 CP.
  • Next, two cards will be selected. Based on the realm and rarity of these cards, you'll either get an HR-L card or items!
  • You'll also get a bonus based on the number of stars you get. Accumulate more stars and get even more bonus presents.
  • Lastly, exchange the DP you get from purchasing the CP for sweet cards.
