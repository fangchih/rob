About Specialist Fist Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Power up your deck with ATK/DEF skill cards!


Guaranteed S Rare - Legend ATK/DEF skill card

on your third and fifth draws!

Completion Rewards[edit]

Draw both packs five times and receive an SS

Rare - Legend ATK/DEF skill card plus 50

ultimate powders!

Notes[edit]

  • Each draw contains ATK skill cards or DEF skill cards.
  • The 3rd and 5th draws contain one guaranteed S Rare or higher card of that draw's type, either ATK or DEF skill.
  • An additional ATK/DEF skill card is available by drawing five times from each pack.
  • Completion rewards are sent directly to users Presents.
  • Completion rewards are chosen randomly from the list of included cards.
  • The pack can be reset after getting the completion rewards. This can be done twice.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
