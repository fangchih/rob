About this Event[edit]

In a land of snow and ice, Lovers vow never to let winter end...

Story[edit]

A town suddenly assaulted by freezing weather and a deadly blizzard...
A silver castle, trapped in ice and snow, waiting for you to explore!
What is the truth of the frozen creatures luring in the castle?

What is the Crystal Castle[edit]

  • Explore the Crystal Castle in these quests exclusive to the event period! Get Rare Cards!
  • Cooperate with Fellows to Defeat Powerful Monsters! The Ice Gods, Ghost, Gigas, Ark Dragon and Skadi, are coming for you! Are you ready?!
  • This event also has Order Ranking rewards! You can even get rewards based on the number of monsters you defeat! Get Rank #1 with your Order members!

Snow Crystals[edit]

About Snow Crystals[edit]

  • There are 4 types: White Snow Crystals, Blue Snow Crystals, Pink Snow Crystals, Gold Snow Crystals
  • You will only receive items by defeating Monsters within the 'Blizzard of the Crystal Castle' Event.
  • The Snow Crystals you receive are based on the amount of ATK PWR used when you successfully defeat the monster
  • If the Monster flees, even if you use ATK PWR you will not be rewarded with snow crystals
  • According to the type of Monster you defeat, the type of Snow Crystal you receive will differ.

Exchange Information[edit]

Exchange them to get 1 random item from those below!

Cards and Treasures you can get in the Event Quests are exclusive to the event. You can get cards or Treasures in all areas.
