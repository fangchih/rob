Changes from Past Holy Wars[edit]

The following changes are planned for this Holy Wars event:
(Please note: plans may change without notice. Thank you for understanding.)



Declaring War[edit]

  1. Holy Wars are held according to a set schedule.
  2. You can delcare war by tapping the 'Declare War' button from the Holy Wars screen.
  3. Anybody in your Order can declare war.
  4. War declarations apply to a whole day of wars, not individual wars.
  5. You can't fight in wars that occur on a day for which you haven't declared war.
  6. Also, you can't cancel war declarations.
  7. In order to participate in every war, you must declare war every day.
  8. You can't specify which Order you want to fight.


Victory Point Reward[edit]

  1. Instead of the usual Victory Rewards, this Holy Wars will feature Victory Point Rewards.
  2. Rewards will be distributed based on the number of Victory Points that users collect.
  3. On the first and last days, you'll get twice the number of Victory Points as normal.


Holy War Coins[edit]

You can get a Holy War Card Pack by using either 1 Gold or 3 Silver Coins.

Getting Gold Coins[edit]

  1. You will receive Gold Coins by getting a set amount of Holy War Points. The amount of Gold Coins you receive will increase according to the amount of Holy War Points you accumulate.
  2. If you lose in a Holy War you will not receive any Gold Coins.
  3. Listed below are the required amounts of Holy War Points to receive Gold Coins:
    • 5,000 Points = 1 Coin
    • 10,000 Points = 2 Coins
    • 20,000 Points = 3 Coins
    • 30,000 Points = 4 Coins
    • 40,000 Points = 5 Coins
    • 50,000 Points = 6 Coins
    • 60,000 Points = 7 Coins
    • 72,500 Points = 8 Coins
    • 85,000 Points = 9 Coins
    • 100,000 Points = 10 Coins

Getting Silver Coins[edit]

  1. You accumulate Silver Coins by participating in Holy Wars.
  2. Compete within your Order to accumulate the most Holy War Points. You will receive Silver Coins according to your place within the Order in one Holy War.
  3. You cannot share Silver Coins with players not within your Order.
  4. You will get 1 Silver Coin for every 3,000 Holy War Points you accumulate in 1 Holy War.
  5. You'll get up to 300 Silver Coins from one Holy War.


Sharing Silver Coins[edit]
  1. By sharing Silver Coins with an Order Member, you and the recipient will both receive a Silver Coin.
  2. You can share 1 Silver Coin per Order Member per Holy War. This will be reset during every Holy War.
  3. You cannot share Silver Coins unless you have a shareable amount.
  4. The amount of Silver Coins you can share will be based on the the amount of Silver Coins you have accumulated by participating in a Holy War.
  5. The number of times you can send Silver Coins is reset every Holy War.


After the event has ended and a certain amount of time has passed, Gold Coins and Silver Coins will disappear.

Badge Bonus[edit]

  1. During Holy Wars, Badges are sent to users who get at least 1,000 Individual Holy War Points from one Holy War.
  2. You can get a 'Badge Bonus' based on how many Badges you get.
  3. In order to get every Badge Bonus, you must participate in every Holy War and get at least 1,000 Holy War Points in each one.
  4. After the collection period, even if you have Badges you won't be able to exchange them for bonuses.
