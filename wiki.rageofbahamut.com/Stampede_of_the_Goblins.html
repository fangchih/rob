Story[edit]

In the age of conflict between man, demons, and gods, another race lived in a distant land.

Scheming as the forces of the three realms fought, the race built up their army and waited patiently.

Called 'goblins,' they were hated by man, the gods, and even demons. But no longer are they doomed to obscurity and insignificance. The curtain has risen on the goblin's rebellion.

When their pent-up rage explodes, the goblins will swarm out of their homeland and scorch the earth.

As the goblin horde prepares to ravage the surface, the knights stand ready against them.

Will they be able to stem the stampede?

Stampede of the Goblins: Be prepared. For they are coming...


How to Play This Event[edit]

Battle against monsters with ten-card decks!

By defeating monsters, you will open the Door to the Goblin Lair, where a hidden boss awaits!

Cooperate with your friends to defeat the boss and get rewards!

Final Half of this Event[edit]

Reset Box[edit]

For Silver Goblinite/Silver Goblin Coins Mystery box:

  • When you get Muse, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.

For Gold Goblinite/Silver Goblin Coins Mystery box:

  • When you get Spartacus, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.


Caution

  • Even if the Results screen is not displayed due to an error, the item could still have been sent normally. Please check Presents to see whether you received your items.
  • If you have drawn all of the contents in the box, you will not receive a Present.
