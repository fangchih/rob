Summary[edit]

Available: 7 AM Dec. 29th, 2012 to 7 AM Jan. 14th, 2013

Many limited-edition cards make a reappearance in this New Year's BB! Now is your chance to get your hands on awesome cards you may have missed out on!

  • Regular cards also appear.
  • The Mad Tea Party cards do not appear.
