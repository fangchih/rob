Story[edit]

Your old friends, Anne and Grea, are in the midst of

a magical competition with their rivals, Leonard

Academy.


Having lost the first of a three round match, Mysteria

is desperate for a victory. Fight alongside Anne and

grasp glory in the upcoming summoner tower race.


P.S. Lou's hard at work cracking on the books. Please

help watch over her studies.


How to Play[edit]

Gather echoes to make echo seals Your order will get a big bonus to lost memories! Battle lots of raid bosses to up the bonus!

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixirs, swirlpops, and yummy treats are available for free on the event screen once every day.

The daily event bonus can be claimed once every 24 hours, starting from 1 AM (ET)

Bonus High Rares Just for Playing![edit]

Visit the main event screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available when you proceed through the event; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 ultimate powders (personal), which let you hit raid bosses with 16 times the damage of a 1-AP attack! Claim them from your Presents after the event starts.

You can change the ultimate powders (personal) into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.

If the event preview gets enough likes and shares on facebook you'll get even more ultimate powders (personal) when the event starts.
