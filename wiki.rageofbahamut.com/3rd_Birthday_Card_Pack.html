About 3rd Birthday Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

3rd Birthday SS Rare and Legend cards! Use friendship points and Premium CP tickets to draw cards from the box!


Ultra[edit]

Draws Cost Contents
1 2700 RageMedals 10 H Rare-Legend Cards from the box
30 Holy Powders
3 Tablet Fragments
30 Bazaar Tickets

Get with Friendship Points[edit]

Draws Cost Contents
1 600 Friendship Points 1 H Rare-Legend Cards from the box
10 6000 Friendship Points 10 H Rare-Legend Cards from the box

Get with Premium CP Tickets[edit]

Draws Cost Contents
1 1 Premium Card Pack Ticket 1 H Rare-Legend Cards from the box
1 Personal Holy Powder
3 Bazaar Tickets
10 10 Premium Card Pack Tickets 10 H Rare-Legend Cards from the box
10 Personal Holy Powders
30 Bazaar Tickets

Notes[edit]

  • You may not purchase the Ultra tier when there are less than ten cards or when there are no more H Rare - Legend cards left in the box.
  • Animations may be cut cut short due to issues with your network connectiion.
  • If the results shown in the animation differ from those on the results screen, the results on the results screen are correct.
  • Even if the animations are skipped, you should receive your cards or items. Check your Presents.
