About Epic Card Pack

How To Play

Obtain cards from the desired realm and

defeat the boss to get additional rewards!

Notes

  • Cards obtained from additional rewards belong to the realm of the card pack purchased.
  • Damage is only dealt to the enemy character belonging to the realm of the card pack purchased.
  • Cards from additional rewards do not contribute to the damage dealt to the enemy character.
  • Additional rewards are only obtained when the health of the enemy character drops below the listed percentage.
  • Once the enemy character of a particular realm has been defeated, users can no longer purchase card packs belonging to that realm.
