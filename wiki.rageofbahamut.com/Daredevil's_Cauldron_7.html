How To Play[edit]

Raise Your Streak or Lose Everything!

Sacrifice cards to raise your streak! Lose your cards and rewards if you fail... The higher the streak, the greater the reward.

Each Sacrifice yields cauldron points, which you can collect for rewards!


Details[edit]

Sacrificing cards to the cauldron will yield cauldron points. The greater the rarity of the card you sacrifice, the higher the chance that you'll raise your success streak. The highest rarity you can sacrifice is an S Rare.

Success Streaks
Your streak may rise each time you sacrifice a card and you succeed.
Fail and you lose everything and your success streak will be reset to zero.
You can get up to a 30-success streak.
Success Streak Rewards
The quality of your success streak reward is based on how high your success streak is.
When you exchange your success streak for a reward, your success streak will reset to zero.
Cauldron Points
You'll receive cauldron points each time you sacrifice a card.
Cauldron Point Rewards
You'll receive rewards in your Presents as you collect cauldron points and reach certain thresholds.
Rarity Success Rate Cauldron Points
N 60 1
HN 65 4
R 70 16
HR 75 64
SR 85 256
  • You cannot sacrifice cards SS Rare and above.

Notes[edit]

  • The card you sacrifice will disappear regardless of whether you raise your success streak or not.
  • You may fail when sacrificing cards to the cauldron.
  • The streak is raised by one each time you succeed.
  • Some rewards come in limited numbers. If the highest rewards you are eligible for has already been claimed by others, you will receive the next-highest reward instead.
