Polymer Witch's name.. -- GlacierLord (talk) 05:20, 8 August 2013 (EDT) -- GlacierLord (talk) 05:20, 8 August 2013 (EDT)&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Her name translation could either be Poliahu a hawaiian snow goddess (her lore hints to this), and her final stage should be "Snow Goddess" Witch Poliahu. However her skill name makes reference to Ziegler a german chemist famed for his work on Polymers, and since her name could be kanji for the katakana of "Polymahoru" or Polymer, and Ziegler means "brick-make" or "brick-maker" and the heavy german theme of the card even she was released in, it is more likely she is an Ice Polymer Witch, and her skill is Ziegler Ice Curse (or Ziegler Affliction Ice/Brick-Make Ice Malediction...Though could possibly translate as Ziegler Ice Cream XD).

Went with the most logical based on the sway of the card art, theme of related cards in the same cardpack and raid event (Arc of St. Time, a heavy winter and ice themed event that introduced Snow White as well). The lore points the opposite direction as her being the Hawaiian Snow Goddess Poliahu though so it might be a joke or play on words because her name could go to Either Polymer Witch or Poliahu (likely a joke/play on words for them, aka double entandre).
