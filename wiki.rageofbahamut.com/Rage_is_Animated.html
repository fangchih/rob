Story[edit]

Rivenbrandt, a world where man, gods, and

demons salt the earth...


Recorded in ancient history was a great battle,

one pitting Bahamut the obsidian-winged

dragon against the combined might of the three

races. The world was pushed to the brink of

annihilation, but with a heavy cost the coalition

managed to seal the dragon into a fitful

slumber. Two keys were required to break the

seal. One went to the demons, the other to the

gods. Both were meant to be separated for

eternity, never to reunite.


Two thousand peaceful years have passed. But

the flow of time has eroded the memories of many.


The men, Bahamut has become a divine being, a

fearful deity. In the upper realm, the key held

by the gods has been stolen. The dragon's

second coming nears, threatening to plunge the

world into madness.


Veiled by shadows, a figure yearns for the end

of the world.

"Apocalypse, bring your judgment down upon

this cursed land."

Characters[edit]

[Wild Arrow] Favaro Leone
Favaro is a bounty hunter who lives for
thrills, women, and booze. His wild side
comes from his father, a pirate. Dirty
fighting is a mastered art, one he'll never
apologize for. A whimsical lie throws him
into the adventure of a lifetime.

[Fallen Knight] Kaisar Lidfard
Kaisar is a man of noble blood, a knight of
high morals ans standards. An encounter
with Favaro leaves his reputation in tatters,
condemning the knight to a life of bounty
hunting. His only faithful companion is his
horse.

[Key Bearer] Amira
Amira fell from the sky, holding a key stolen
from the gods. Forces pursue her as she aims
for Helhein, a region in the north. Within
her eyes is a mysterious power.
