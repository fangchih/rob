Description[edit]

Card packs are another way for players to obtain cards.

There are various types for players to get, ranging from free daily packs, event only packs, and paid packs which are occasionally given free for events.

Friendship Card Pack[edit]

Friendship Card Pack will cost players 200 Friendship Points.
Friendship Card Pack contains cards from rarity Normal to Rare.
It's possible to obtain a High Rare card from the Friendship Pack, but the chances are very low.
Players will also receive a free Friendship Card Pack daily.

Card list of cards obtained from the Friendship Card Pack.

Silver Coins Card Pack[edit]

Silver Coins Card Pack costs players 3 Silver Coins.
Silver Coins Card Pack contains cards from rarity Normal, High Normal, Rare, High Rare, to ?.

Players will receive Silver Coins for contribution during the Holy War, players can also 'give' silver coins to others in their Order, effectively doubling your number of coins since you keep yours when giving.
When silver coins are given, you will receive another Silver Coin if you choose to send a message along with it, effectively tripling the amount of coins received via Holy War.

Card list that obtained from Silver Coins Card Pack.

Premium Card Pack (Premium CP)[edit]

Also known as Legendary, Legend Card Packs, or LCP

Near the end of 2012, Cygames changed Legend CP to Premium CP. You may see in some areas of this site, Legend, or LCP, still being used.

As of 26 November, 2013, Premium CPs were changed to give 1 Holy Powder(Personal) instead of 1 Holy Powder.

Premium Card Packs cost players 300 RageMedals (100 on the first pack).
Premium Card Packs contains cards from the rarity High Normal to SSRare.
(Though many players complain about the inclusion of High Normal cards in a pack which costs real world currency.)
Sometimes they will have a higher chance of dropping SR and SSR or drop only Rares & UP when advertized.

Players will receive a total of 4 Claim Tickets for Premium Card Pack as they progress through the game. One after the defeat of the first boss, one after reaching Level 20, two after reaching Level 40.
Premium Card packs are commonly included in presents for events.

Premium Card Packs are also sold in Box of 6, at the price of 5.
Each Pack also will include an item on special occasions.

Was also available for a limited time as part of the Million Users Celebration Campaign, which has already ended.
Can be obtained from the 3 Million+ Users Campaign. Until Apr. 1, 2014 3 Premium Card Packs were available in current stamp login campaigns. (Exactly the same as the 3 Million+ Users Campaign, but no additional Dark Angel Olivia)Apr. 2, 2014 they got replaced with Reward Card Packs (see below). Since Jan. 15th, 2015, the Reward Card Packs in the log in campaign once again got replayed by Premium CP. Therefore no more Free HP is available in the Campaign.

Note: Premium CP and Legend CP may be used for each other on this site until all cards, card templates, can be updated.

Card list that obtained from Premium Card Pack included below guarantee card pack.

Reward Card Pack[edit]

Shortly after changing HP to HP (Personal) in Premium Card Packs (see above), Reward Card Packs were introduced into the game. They contain 1 Holy Powder, 1 Card and 3 Bazaar Tickets - as Premium Card Packs did previous to 26th November 2013. As of Apr. 2nd, 2014, Reward Card Packs have replaced Premium Card Packs in current stamp login campaigns. As of Jan. 15th, 2015 Reward Card Packs in the stamp login campaign were once again replaced by Premium Car Packs. Currently no more Free HP are therefor available.

Reward Card Packs are obtainable in event rewards, such as event boxes.

Rare & Up Pack[edit]

A virtually unobtainable card pack, though not because of its desirability. Only available via Battle Ranking rewards.
Essentially the same as the Legendary Card pack when they guaranteed rare & up.
Available via Battle Ranking rewards in exchange for 50 Gems.

High Rare & Up Pack[edit]

Typically available as a reward for placing #6 - #100 in Order Ranking during Holy Wars, though #6 - #10 sometimes will receive the S Rare & Up Pack reward instead.
Also received via referred friend reaching level 50.
Also available via Battle Ranking rewards in exchange for 200 Gems.

S Rare & Up Pack[edit]

Typically available as a reward for placing #1 - #5 in Order Ranking during Holy Wars, though #6 - #10 sometimes will also receive this reward.
Quest-oriented Events will also receive this reward, though only through individual rankings.
Also available via Battle Ranking rewards in exchange for 800 Gems.

SS Rare & Up Pack[edit]

Virtually unobtainable as the only way to get this pack is through the Battle Ranking rewards.
Available via Battle Ranking rewards in exchange for 2000 Gems.


Rulers of the Azure Packs[edit]

Rulers of the Azure BB will cost 3000 RageMedals for each box. And 1500 for the half-box
Rulers of the Azure BB come in 10 or 5 cards.
Each Rulers of the Azure BB will contain 3 Azure CP Claim Tickets and 10 Bazaar Tickets depending on the BB purchased.
The 5 card BB gives 1 Azure CP Claim Ticket and 5 Bazaar Tickets.
