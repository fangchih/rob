General Info[edit]

  • Daily log in to get a stamp.
  • You get presents depending on the number of stamps you have collected.
  • This campaign will continue even if you cannot log in some days.
  • You will only get stamps for the days you log in.

Notes[edit]

  • A campaign normally starts on the first day each month and lasts till the 10th day.
  • At the 14/15th each month a new campaign will start and last until the last day of the month.
  • After gaining 10 stamps in a campaign, you will receive one Angel Queen and 1,000 Friendship Points for each of the following stamps until the next campaign starts.
  • Please note, that the Reward Card Packs you'd obtain in each of this campaigns go directly into your presents and are - like the rest of the Login Bonuses only claimable when you register your device.
