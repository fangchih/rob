Synopsis[edit]

Seize magical treasures back from monsters in order to prevent the Easter Egg from hatching!

Quest[edit]

There are a total of 100 quest, broken down to 10 stages with 5 quest each forming up 1 chapter.
In short, every 5 quest equates to a chapter cleared and every chapter ends with a boss fight.

As you climb up further, it takes more hits to clear a quest, thus requiring a lot more stamina for later stages.

  • NOTE

After the initial 100 quest, you can keep on clearing the quest to fight for placing.
However for the subsequent quest boss fight, the only reward are rupies.
Thus making it a rupie or treasure run. Thou you can still get the rare cards (Angel Queen, Gold Dragon or Devil Queen)

Boss Fight[edit]

Boss Fight occurs after every 5 quest, signifying the end of a chapter.

This boss fight is akin to the "The Attack of the Giants" event, but with the mechanics similar to how normal quest end boss fight.

Battle party is form up by your leader card and 2 more fellow's leader card from the respective alignment you choose.
If you have fellows or order members within the same stage they will join you in your party. The boss fight then will commence.

Event Skills such as Stolas, Odysseus's or Cyclops's skill will be activated in this fight.

The boss fight will be the same as fighting the cyclops. Each strike from the boss will remove a party member, thus reducing your next strike damage on the boss.

The boss fight at the end of Area 50 and Area 100 will be a special boss.
Defeating him will grant you a Rare card

Treasures & New Cards[edit]

Keepers of the Easter Egg is an event which put players through 100 (and more) quest, in which there are 3 different sets of treasure to obtain.

  • Flowery Egg
  • Faberge Egg
  • Easter Lily Pendant
  • Flowery Egg+
  • Faberge Egg+
  • Easter Lily Pendant+


This treasures can be obtained via battle as well, thus making all 3 Items huge in demand. Treasures may not be placed on wishlist, thus you cannot send it as a gift, reducing it down to only 2 methods to obtain them. Also confirmed is that you will only unlock 2 treasure in quest, thus rendering the only way to complete the whole series is by battle.

The colours of treasures you can obtained in quest is limited to the Realm you are.

  • Man : Red and Blue Treasure
  • God : Yellow and Green Treasure
  • Demon : Emerald and Purple Treasure


Each sets of treasure will unlock a High Rare card of an alignment (You may refer to the Card List for details)

  • Flowery Egg : Man High Rare Card (Rabbitteer Knight)
  • Faberge Egg : God High Rare Card (Angel Bunny)
  • Easter Lily Pendant : Demon High Rare Card (Bunny Lilith Limil)
  • Flowery Egg+ : Man High Rare Card (Rabbitteer Knight+)
  • Faberge Egg+ : God) High Rare Card (Angel Bunny+)
  • Easter Lily Pendant+ : Demon High Rare Card (Bunny Lilith Limil+)

The difference between this cards from the normal treasure unlocked cards is that they can be evolved.

Also with this event, there are several new High Normal cards to be unlocked.

  • Lady Gunner (Man)
  • Pegasus (God)
  • Thunderbird (God)
  • Phantom (Demon)

A New type of Rare Stats improvement card

  • Devil Queen (Demon)


This card levels up card skills

At selected stage there will be rare cards to be unlocked.

  • Gold Fox (Rare God) When you clear Quest 50 & Quest 100
