Story[edit]

Returning with the sick Vania to the Castle in the Sky the Hero meets a mysterious girl called Spinaria

She knows more than she lets on.

The secrets of Vania's lost memories are enclosed within her hands...

As the party approaches the heart of the Castle, things become murkier...

Vania's origin is not the only obscured truth. Within the castle lurks a secret that could shatter the entire world...


How to Play This Event[edit]

Defeat bosses to get items!

Partner up with your favorite teammate

Win exclusive event cards!

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Spinaria crunches and fragrant flowers are available for free on the Event screen once every day.

  • Spinaria crunches recovers one AP used to attack raid bosses.
  • You can exchange fragrant flowers by tapping Gift Flowers on the Rewards tab of the event screen
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed once every 24 hours, starting from 5 AM (ET).

Free Card[edit]

Visit the main event screen during Egg of Decimation to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available in the quest. Evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Flower Exchange[edit]

  • There are three types of rewards: Unlimited, Limited, and Gold Limited.
  • When you exchange Fragrant Flowers, you may get either Limited box presents or Unlimited ones.
  • However, when you exchange Snow Flowers, you'll always get Limited box presents.
  • Fragrant and Snow Flowers share the same box. Safira can only be drawn through exchanging Unearthly Jack-o'-Lanterns.
  • Blood Flowers are exchanged from a separate box. You'll always get limited box presents and Ectis can only be drawn from this box.
  • You can receive up to 20 Spinaria Crunches every 24 hours, starting from 5 AM (ET)
Refilling the Box
Fill the conditions written for each box to refill it with the contents of the next box until you reach Box 4.
