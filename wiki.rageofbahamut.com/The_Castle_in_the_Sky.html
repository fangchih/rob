About this event[edit]

Event Details[edit]

The path to the legendary Castle in the Sky has opened! Solve its riddles while collecting every Treasure along the way

About The Castle in the Sky[edit]

  • There will be quests for a limited time! Get Rare cards by completing Treasure Series
  • Raise a Fairy with the other members in your Order! What will the rewards be for raising her...?!
  • The highest-ranking users will get SS Rare Cards, so keep progressing through the quests and aim for the top!

Story[edit]

The Simbel Mountains were regarded by their inhabitants as a 'road to heaven.'
Legend had it that this 'road to heaven' traveled through the summit an up to a castle floating in the sky.
Your king has a pressing request--that you wander the heavens in search of this castle and unlock its mysteries.
With no choice, your group sets off in order to explore this ancient castle in the sky...
Who--or what built this floating garden?
What of the fairies said to have lived there and the once-flourishing town that now lies in ruins?
Can you uncover their secrets?

Notes[edit]

This is a limited-time quest event.

Event Quests vs Main Quests[edit]

Once completed, the event quests cannot be played again.

Treasures exclusive to this event will appear. Cards and Treasures that you can obtain in this event are not disclosed beforehand, so keep playing to find out what they are! Allies can cooperate (see below) to receive Progress Bonuses. Huge bonuses appear in certain areas. You can obtain cards by defeating certain level bosses.

You can get a bonus card if you have completed all Treasure series when you complete Quest 100.

Reward for the completion of Chapters and Mystic Treasures. Please note that upon meeting the requirements for the rewards (Completion of 100 Chapters and gathering all Mystic Treasures) you will not be able to receive them until you have proceeded to the event quest page to view the flash animation. Once you have viewed the animation, the rewards should be sent to directly to your ‘Presents’ page.

Event Treasure[edit]

Event Treasure can only be obtained in these exclusive quests during the event time period. They cannot be obtained through the regular quests.

In addition, once this event period ends, they cannot be obtained freshly through quests. However, they can be seized in battles even after this event ends. Unlike normal Treasure, these cannot be gifted to other users. Event-exclusive cards can be obtained by completing Event Treasure series.

Ally Cooperation[edit]

If your Fellows or other members in your Order (known as Allies) also play this event, you will receive a Co-op Bonus when you enter an Area close to where an Ally is playing, and your Progress will go up. (This bonus is added automatically) You can receive up to 1 Co-op Bonus a day from each Ally.

About boss battles[edit]

You can call two Fellows to fight with you. In addition the system will select two from your Order. When you beat a boss, Allies who fought with you will receive Friendship Points. Cards that have Event skills do have a chance of activating. The bosses at level 50 and level 100 are somewhat harder, just like in Tears of the Moon.

About raising Fairies[edit]

Orders each raise 1 Fairy. You can raise a Fairy individually if you are not an Order member. However, in that case you cannot receive rewards based on Order rank. Order Contribution Levels are based on Growth Points obtained and are reset when you enter or change Orders. Only Order leaders or individual users who aren't Order members can change Fairy names. The Fairy level max is 50. They won't grow past that, but obtained Growth Points will count toward individual and Order scores.

About presents from Fairies:[edit]

When Fairies level up and change form, all Order members receive a present. However, you will not receive a present if your Order Contribution Level is below a certain amount.

Check the Fairy's growth on the Fairy Diary page to have the present sent to your Presents.

Once presents are received, you cannot receive duplicate presents if you change Orders. You also receive a present each time the Heart meter fills (1,000 growth points). At certain special places (for example 10,000 growth points) the meter will fill multiple times.

Present contents are random.

About Individual Growth Point Ranking[edit]

Individuals compete in this ranking to get the most Growth Points for raising their Fairies.

About Growth Point Ranking[edit]

Orders compete in this ranking to get the most Growth Points. If you enter/change Orders during the event, the Growth Points counted toward your Order will

Scores will take approximately 30 minutes to be reflected in rankings. In addition, if multiple users have the same number of Growth Points, the one who achieved them faster will be ranked higher. Individuals who don't make the rankings will not receive rewards.
