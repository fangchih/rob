About[edit]



Orders are similar to guilds in other mmorpg's in that they also provide a separate community for it's members and have a hierarchy. There are several advantages for being a member of an order such as a slight bonus to ATK/DEF. Each order has an information page which provides some basic information such as the current level of the order, the current leader, the number of members currently in the order and the total number of rupies collected by the order. You can also request an invite by visiting this page.

Once you are a member of an order you will also be able to view the order forum and it's current size. You can communicate with your other order members and choose to donate Treasures or Rupies to help increase the size of your order. You can only be a member of one order at a time.

Orders can be created by anyone with a level of 20 or higher and if they have 10 or more Fellows.

Furthermore, the leader of an order has some special powers, such as assigning ranks, deciding who can join and building Order Buildings that increase members' ATK/DEF. As of Holy Wars 8 (Rebirth), Vice-Leaders will now be able to build walls.
