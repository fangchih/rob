Time Period(ET): 12 AM June 10th, 2013 to 9 PM June 15th 2013

Card Pack Details[edit]

Draw cards to attack the skeleton. Defeat them and get strong cards.

You will attack the skeletons with the cards you draw. Once the HP of the skeletons reach 0, they will be defeated. There are a total of three skeletons on each round. There are four total rounds. You will go back to the first round after defeating all of the skeletons in round four.

The stronger the cards you draw, the more damage you can do to the skeletons. There are also cards with special effects that will do extra damage to the skeletons.

A bonus card may drop when you defeat a skeleton. When you defeat King of Bones in round 4 you are guaranteed to get an SSR card Fortuna as a reward.
