Q: For evolved forms of cards, are the listed stats supposed to represent 0 enhancement of a 4Normal evolution?

A: Current edited stats seem to be inconsistent with in game values. Currently values for evolved forms are shown lower than the lowest possible. Needs to be addressed/clarified.

EXAMPLE: Card = Knight

(Current shown stats on this wiki)

lvl 1 Knight______________: 350ATK 450DEF

lvl 1 Knight+_____________: 456ATK 586DEF

lvl 1 Knight++____________: 490ATK 630DEF

lvl 1 [SILVER GUARD]Knight: 630ATK 810DEF

(In game 4Normal evolve with NO enhancement of any stages)

lvl 1 Knight______________: 350ATK 450DEF

lvl 1 Knight+_____________: 456ATK 586DEF (evolve 2 lvl 1 Knights)

lvl 1 Knight++____________: 531ATK 683DEF (evolve lvl 1 Knight+ with lvl 1 Knight)

lvl 1 [SILVER GUARD]Knight: 675ATK 868DEF (evolve lvl 1 Knight++ with lvl 1 Knight)

Re: Order Crusher Points -- Darrick966 (talk) 02:31, 29 December 2013 (EST) -- Cormyre (talk) 20:01, 29 December 2013 (EST)&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

To track inactives within an order I've had to create spreadsheets and log every battle in CC and HW.

With CC you have to go into rankings after every battle (have to wait 30 minutes on a win for bonuses to be applied, can log immediately if not) and log the current points for each member and subtract it from the last to find out if they were active.

With HW after the win/loss screen comes up it shows a list of participants and point totals.

Hope that helped.

Re: how to add lores to cards? and whats y'all rob igns? -- BestSushi (talk) 09:17, 17 November 2014 (EST) -- Morgan (talk) 11:09, 17 November 2014 (EST)&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Hello and welcome to wiki. To add lore text to wiki, just click "Edit" on the card you want to edit, scroll down the card date until you find the entry "|lore1 = " and start typing. the number behind lore indicates the evolution step of the card with 1 being base, 2 being +, 3 = ++ and 4= Final form. when you're done, press the save button and you have successfully added the lore text. If you've got other questions regarding editing cards, just feel free to ask. :) --Morgan (talk) 11:09, 17 November 2014 (EST)
