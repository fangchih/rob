Story[edit]

A single beam of blue light
cut through the dark, mist-
shrouded heavens of Valhalla.

Soon,
twilight enveloped the noonday sky,
and across the earth,
fiery tendrils reached skyward.

Fallen warriors of old,
brandishing dark power
in the hands of their illusory bodies,
set upon the living.

Those iniquitous spirits
doused the world in flame,
and the world wailed
a terrible cry to demarcate the end.

It was then.

One Valkyrie warrior
alighted to this infernal world
and came to you,
bearing but one hope in her breast...


How to Play this Event[edit]

Defeat the approaching raid bosses with a ten-card deck! By defeating the required raid bosses, you can open the Gates to Valhalla, giving you the opportunity to spawn a hidden boss! Cooperate with your friends to defeat the boss and get rewards!


Free AP Recovery Items[edit]

In Gotterdammerung, you get a special event participation bonus every day! By going to the event top screen, you will receive dusk berries once daily. Use these to vanquish raid bosses and get rewards!

  • AP is used when you attack raid bosses.
  • The event participation bonus is reset daily at 6 AM (ET)

First Half[edit]

Once the first half of the event is over, you will get an individual reward based on your interim rankings! Special cards to help you through the second half of the event might be available too!

First Half ends: Mar. 27th, 2013 at 10:00 PM (ET)

Once First Half ends, you will no longer receive White Dusk Leafs or White Dusk Flowers. Remember to exchange these before the end of the event.

Reset Box[edit]

For White Dusk Leafs/White Dusk FLowers Mystery box:

  • When you get Enduring Angel, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.

For Gold Dusk Leafs/Gold Dusk Flowers Mystery box:

  • When you get Majestic Dancer, you'll be able to reset the contents of the box.
  • The box can be reset up to 3 times.


Caution

  • Even if the Results screen is not displayed due to an error, the item could still have been sent normally. Please check Presents to see whether you received your items.
  • If you have drawn all of the contents in the box, you will not receive a Present.


Second Half[edit]

Second Half will start (given that there is no complications like when this event started) after a brief 'Event Maintenance' period on Mar. 27th, 2013.
