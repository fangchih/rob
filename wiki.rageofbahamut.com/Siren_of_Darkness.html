Story[edit]

After the events of "Prophet's Vision"...

Just when the hero and their companions

finally reached the prophet, they were

swallowed up by a parallel world controlled

by the Hollow Chaos... known as the Dark

Realm to find Reno and Kit, lost and wondering

too. Banding together, they try to make their

escape, but a most evil foe blocks their way...


How to Play[edit]

Defeat bosses to get items! Use Items to train Sandy!

Some items enhance ATK, others DEF!

The more you use, the better she gets!

Cards awarded after the event!


Rank on top of your grouping every day

for a legend CP Claim Ticket!

New Features[edit]

Atk & Def Tomes
You may receive Attack and Defense Tomes as MVP Rewards or as Final Blow Rewards for defeating a rare raid boss.
You may also receive them as Daily Mission Rewards.
(Note: Tomes are no longer granted as dmg rewards!)
Skill Tomes
You may receive Skill Tomes as MVP Rewards or Golden Rewards for defeating a rare raid boss.
You may also receive them as Special Rewards or Daily Mission Rewards.


Sandy[edit]

  • Train Sandy using the tomes and spheres that raid bosses drop when defeated.
  • Users who train the Sandy a lot will get a special Legend card at the end of the event. Users who didn't will still get a significantly weaker SS Rare card, but it won't be as good. Wizards's exact stats, alignment, and evolution depend on the type and number of items used in training.


Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours.

  • One Elixir recovers 1 AP during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the Dragoon's Dilemma page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.
