Other Items&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Maze Powder&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Usage: Use these powders to deal 2x damage to all monsters who appear in The Sylvan Labyrinth Event! Gives Silver Maze Flower(s) when you attack.

Obtain from:

  • The Sylvan Labyrinth Special Rewards
  • The Sylvan Labyrinth Flower Exchange
  • Shop costs 100 Rage Medals or MobaCoins; Special deals available

Note: Once The Sylvan Labyrinth event ends, all remaining Maze Powder(s) will be converted to Holy Powder.

Evanescent Holy Powder&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Evanescent Holy Powder

Usage: Fully recovers ATK PWR and DEF PWR only a period of Holy War. Cannot be traded.

Obtain from: Logging in in a period before Holy Wars which follow by rule of each Holy Wars.

Note: As of Holy Wars 8 (Rebirth), the preparation period only gives 1 Holy Powder (Personal) in place of Evanescent Holy Powder.

Dragon Powder&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Usage: Fully recovers ATK PWR and DEF PWR, damage of members is increased up to 1.5 times.

Obtain from: Shop during the past event The Roar of the Dragons, costs 100 Rage Medals
