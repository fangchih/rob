hey, this happens when the card is already there. Mostly with older cards, since they often just uploaded the pictures whichever format they had. When you use the new form (link above) you have to make sure, that the Pictures are named after the card. Sometimes this means you have to download an old picture, convert it to .jpg and reupload it. But most of the time it's just a different name( like: .JPG instead of .jpg) or the numbers behind the name are wrong. You can then just open the picture from the old page, before you change anything and Move them to the new name with the dropdown menu left of the "search" bar. I hope this helps.
