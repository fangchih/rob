About this Event[edit]

Something has enchanted our forest, turning it into a maze... Can you help us investigate?

What is the Manor of Illusions?[edit]

  • There will be quests for a limited time! Get Rare cards by completing Treasure Series!
  • The Manor of Illusions has many paths to choose! Cooperate with your Order to find the right one!
  • Collect the Pumpkin Cookies that the Quests drop to get Ranking rewards!

Story[edit]

In a certain forest...
Your party was stumbling through the dense wilderness looking for a way to the next town.
Your surroundings got darker and darker the further you went.
Just as the sun was setting and everything was turning pitch-black, you noticed something strange.
No matter how far you go, you keep ending up in the same spot.
Suddenly the path in front of you lights up and a gigantic manor appears!
In order to make it out of this forest you must investigate this manor.
What's waiting for you in the depths of the Manor of Illusions?
