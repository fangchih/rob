Getting trade offers

From Rage of Bahamut WIKI
Jump to: navigation, search

To get some good trade offers you can do the following:

Join the Forums[edit]

  1. A great place to start is Gamefaqs or ROBforums.com

Support spree[edit]

  1. Update your wishlist
  2. Go to one of your fellows and support them
  3. You then land back on the user's page, and go to the user that made a comment just before you
  4. Make a comment on his page also, and repeat the previous step.

This will get you some nice Friendship Points, but your name on a lot of comment pages as well. Because you always go to the most recent comment, you have a big chance the user will still be online, and taking a look at your page.

Good Trading Materials[edit]

If you do not have many Rare or High Rare cards to trade for those you want, here are some tips you can use to try and obtain cards from other players (Also having good manners is always a key point)

  1. Stock up on Angel Queen, Devil Queen or even Gold Dragon. The first 2 are material cards which you can only obtain during events and are good enhance material cards, while the Gold Dragon is fixed at 30,000 Rupies, which some players might want to trade for several cards.
  2. Stock up on Cure Water, Holy Powder and Magic Circle, they are often sought for by higher tier players who will need an abundant of them for up coming events.
  3. Stock up on Normal, High Normal cards, several players will pay to get those cards as well, though the quantity will normally be huge. You can also sell them to players who might be looking for it.
  4. Farm for Rupies and use it to obtain those items or cards above and trade for what you want.

Rule of thumb :
Always be nice, try to negotiate well with other players. If you are in an order, trade within the order if there are people whom you can trust.People also want fair trades such as rares for rares so don't try to give normal cards for rare cards. Also try to get what will help you for something that will help them. Adding on to the fair trade, if a card has similar stats but is lower in rarity, it should be fine.
