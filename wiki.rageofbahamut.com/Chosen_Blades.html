Story[edit]

Rivenbrandt is on the verge of ruin...

Vile creatures thought to have been vanquished long ago are reappearing everywhere, terrorizing the land.

In a last-ditch effort to save the world, the fairy queen Aria has summoned the four Chosen Blades:

Swordsoothing Wind, Demonblade Arrow, Swordgiving Ark, and you, Savior Blade.

How to Play[edit]

Play through the event quest Cooperate to defeat raid bosses! Win exclusive event cards!

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Bunches of fay berries and bronze orbs are available for free on the event screen once every day.

  • Each bunch of fay berries recovers one AP used to attack raid bosses.
  • You can exchange bronze orbs for rewards by tapping Exchange Orbs on the Rewards tab of the event screen.
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed one every 24 hours, starting from 5 AM (ET).

Bonus High Rares Just For Playing![edit]

Visit the main event screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available when you proceed through the event; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 semi-gold powders, which you can use to deal twice the damage of a regular 6 AP attack against raid bosses! Claim them from your Presents after the event starts.

You can change semi-gold powder into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.

Notes[edit]

  • Any user can declare.
  • You don't need to declare to be able to participate in this event.
  • You can pick up your declaration items by visiting the main event screen after the event starts!
