How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade!

Release woodfolks to get items!

Flower Fairy and Grea yield the most!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more panchrestia drops than other brigades.
  • Emerald globes drop when you comfort spirits.
  • This event will have eight daily 19-hour campaigns. The number of emerald globes that brigades collect is calulated per campaign.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 8 PM Jan. 10th, 2015 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

New Features[edit]

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting at the beginning of your campaign.

  • One Elixir recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the Puppet's Revenge page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.

Bonus High Rares Just for Playing![edit]

Visit the main Secrets of the Wood screen during the event to get a free High Rare event card. Three more copies are available in the quest; evolve them all together to make an S Rare event card!

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 6 personal tinctures, which you can use to perfectly incinerate undead beings and boost ATK by 20% in battles against thralls.

You can change personal tinctures into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.
