evo nomenclature&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;n">[edit]&lt;editsection-brackets&gt;

Hi, could you change the evo pattern to how it is used ingame, please. A 8-14 is not a 8-15 and a 4-0 is not a 4-1. The 14 and the 8 in 8-14 describes the max stats possible for this card. Those numbers describe the evolution process itself. The last enhancement doesnt affect the max stats of the card at all and shouldnt be added to the nomenclature. The last enhancement has no effect on the evolution at all.

Actually, the problem is much bigger, because this nomenclature you make use of, sounds like the old one, but isnt. It causes a lot of confusion and missinterpretation, because new player check the wiki and start talking about 8-15 and 4-7 and mix it up with the standard nomenclature used since the game started. They think, that the last number in a 6-6 is simply the amount of max enhances that has been used for the card. But that is not the case. it is the number of max enhances BEFORE final form. you can not max enhance 6 random cards for a 6-6, you have to max enhance the LAST 6 cards you use to evolve it to final form.
