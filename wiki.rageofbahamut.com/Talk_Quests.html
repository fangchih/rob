[2012/07/28] Back with the results on 146 trials. I decided to stop at this point since the numbers should be considered sufficiently large (n>30). With 146 trials, I received 12 Shaman and 134 normals. The mean proportion is 8.22% with a standard deviation of 2.27%. This means that we are 95% confident that the percentage of silver cards comes between 5.95% and 12.77%. To narrow down the SD to 1%, we would need 663 trial runs. To be accurate to the 0.5%, we would need around 2,300 trials. The break-even point for CW usage on stage 18-4 is roughly 750 STA, so anyone that has over 750 STA, it'd be nice if you could do around 700-1000 trials to pick up a more accurate percentage. 18-4 is considered at a significant profit (over 25%) at around 900-1,000 STA. --Valordux
