How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade! Comfort spirits to get items! Cursed spirits yield the most!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more panchrestia drops than other brigades.
  • Emerald globes drop when you comfort spirits.
  • This event will have eight daily 19-hour campaigns. The number of emerald globes that brigades collect is calculated per campaign.
  • You must join a team to participate in this event, so tap the button below to do so!
  • If you don't create or join a team by 9 PM Aug. 4th, 2014 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixir and Precious Rocks are available for free on the event screen once every 24 hours, starting at the beginning of your campaign.

  • One Elixir recovers 30 stamina during the event.
  • Precious Rocks can be exchanged for rewards. On the The Lost Duke page, select the Rewards tab, then tap the Exchange Jewels button.

Declare and Get Bonus Items![edit]

Declare to get 2 personal mending potions, which you can use to perfectly blow up spirits. In battles against wanderers, mending potions boost ATK by 20%.

You can change personal mending potion into holy powder (personal) after the event ends.

All brigades have a chance at the extra killer completion, no matter how many campaigns they win or lose.
