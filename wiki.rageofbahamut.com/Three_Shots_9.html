About Three Shots Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Choose one of three cards in each draw! The last draw on

each tier contains an event card.

Card Pack Content[edit]

Draws Price Contents

9 Rare-Legend Card

Notes[edit]

  • A limited number of packs is available at each price point. Users can purchase up to six Lite, eight Standard, and ten Ultra packs.
  • The deadline to choose a card is 2 PM Jan. 4th (ET).
  • If you can't choose a card before the deadline, the same three cards will appear next time this pack is released. There's not additional charge to choose a card then.
  • Multiple copies of the same card could appear in one draw.
  • At least one event card will appear on the last draw of each tier.

Other Notes[edit]

  • The animation may be cut depending on your connectivity.
  • If the results in the animation are different from the results shown on the result screen, the information displayed on the result screen is correct.
  • Even if the animation is skipped, you'll still get the cards and items that you should. Please check your Presents screen.
