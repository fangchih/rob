How to Play[edit]

Join a six-user team and form a brigade!

Fight bosses and compete to collect the most ranking shards!

Use three sapphire seals to spawn a strong raid boss!

  • In this event, you play in brigades. Each brigade is made up of five 6-user teams who try to collect more ranking shards drops than other brigades.
  • This event will have eight daily 22-hour campaigns. The number of ranking shards that brigades collect is calulated per campaign.
  • If you don't create or join a team by 7 PM Feb. 19th, 2015 (ET), you'll be placed in one after the event starts.


New Features[edit]

Combo
  • A combo will occur when members of your brigade defeat a specific number of raid bosses or quest bosses. Combos let brigade members deal additional damage to the raid bosses.
    • Condition
Defeating 10 of Trial Guard Arboris, Trial Guard Leonis or Trial Gurad Peritus will raise the combo chain by 1.
Defeating 5 of Trial Guard Draco Aetheris will raise combo chain by 1.
Defeating each Lvl ??? Trial Guard Draco Aetheris will raise combo chain by 5.
Defeating any 5 Quest bosses will raise combo chain by 1.
Maximum combo chain will be capped at 100.
    • Combo Period
Combos last for the whole campaign. It will reset at the beginning of each campaign.
    • Combo Effect
Combo chain additional damage will apply to all raid bosses and colossal raid bosses' battles.
Colossal Raid Bosses
  • There are five colossal raid bosses for every brigade. Only one of them can appear in a single campaign.
The main event screen shows a countdown timer indication time to appearance on campaigns with colossal raid bosses.
Not all colossal raid bosses spawn at the same time, even within a specific time slot.
  • Colossal raid bosses spawn in campaign 1, 2, 4, 6 and 8 at the time below:
  • Time Zone A: 9:30pm - 10:00 pm (ET) or 10:10 pm - 10:40 pm (ET)
  • Time Zone B: 5:00pm - 5:30 pm (ET) or 5:40 pm - 6:10 pm (ET)
  • Time Zone C: 9:00pm - 9:30 pm (ET) or 9:40 pm - 10:10 pm (ET)
Wizard
Wizard card (from the Altered Past event) has a skill that deals 15-30 times more damage to colossal raid bosses.
Best Team of Disbanded Brigades
  • For teams in brigades that have lost to the rival brigades at the end of each campaign, if the team's total ranking

shard collected is the highest in the brigade, the team will still get the victory point and killer completion rewards. These conditions must be met:

  • Team's total ranking shards collected for the campaign is higher than the rival brigade's best team in terms of total ranking shards collected.
  • User must meet the minimum required ranking shards for the vitory point and killer completion rewards respectively.

Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Elixirs, Precious Rocks, and Exalted Sapphires are available for free on the event screen once every day

  • Each Elixir recovers 1 AP used to attack raid bosses
  • Precious Rocks and Exalted Sapphires can be exchanged for rewards.
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed once every 24 hours, starting from 6 AM (ET).
