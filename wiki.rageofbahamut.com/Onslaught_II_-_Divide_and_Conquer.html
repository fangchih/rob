Changes from last time[edit]

Last Onslaught Ranking
Rankings from last Onslaught will be displayed on My Page, the Order Screen, the Profile screen and the main Onslaught Screen.
Advanced Declaration Rewards
High Rankers now get better rewards when they declare in advance:
  • Users who did not rank last Onslaught get two personal holy powders.
  • Users who ranked lower than 100 last Onslaught get three personal holy powders.
  • Users who ranked 1000 or higher get four or more personal holy powders, depending on their rank.
Battle Schedule
Onslaught battles now take player at 10AM, 5PM, and 8 PM.
Event rewards
The event rewards have been changed, particularly badge rewards, victory rewards, and victory point rewards.
Countdown Timer
A new countdown timer shows the time left until the next battle. If a battle is already underway, the time remaining is displayed.
Attacks against Walls and Castles
All attacks against walls and castles in Onslaught now count as wins for the attacking user. However, these attacks do not count towards victory chains.
