Fourth Plane Card Pack[edit]

How To Play[edit]

Every pack comes with a bonus!


Draw enough packs and you'll get

four copies of an SS Rare event card

with the maximum LVL and maturity!


About prices[edit]

Price Purchases Contents
Lite 200 36 1 Rare - Legend Card
1 Bonus Set
1 Bazaar Ticket
Ultra 2700 36 10 Rare - Legend Card
1 Bonus Set
10 Bazaar Ticket


Fourth Plane Card Pack Details[edit]

  • There are two different price points.
  • The two price points have different rewards.
  • Browse through the rewards in each plane and select one of the four planes.
  • Purchasing the card pack will yield one reward from your selected plane.
  • The selected plane can be changed at any time.
  • All users have the same rewards in each plane.
  • Get all rewards in four planes in the Lite version and you'll receive fifty mending potions as completion reward.
  • Get all rewards in four planes in the Ultra version and you'll receive a Legend claim Ticket as completion reward.

Other Notes[edit]

  • A quick purchase button is available on the Card Pack Result screen. Pressing the button will allow you to purchase the same pack again.
  • If your selected plane runs out of rewards, a new plane will be automatically selected from the left.
