Story[edit]

In a small port town, Duke Zain prepares to
open his secluded tower to the public for a
momentous all-night ball.

The guest of honor:
your acquaintance, Liza the elf girl.

You make your way though the crowds
ambling toward the tower, but here and
there you hear discomfiting whispers about
the Duke's true character

How to Play This Event[edit]

  • Defeat the boss to get fantastic items!
  • Rescue Liza from the overbearing Duke and his suspicious ball!
  • Collect items and get event-exclusive cards!


Daily Event Bonus[edit]

Cakes and laurel bouquets are available for free on the Event screen once every day.

  • Cakes recover the AP used to attack raid bosses.
  • laurel bouquets can be exchanged for special rewards.
  • The daily event bonus can be claimed once every 24 hours, starting from 6 AM (ET).

Exchange Laurel Bouquet & Rose Bouquet[edit]

  • There are two types of rewards: mystery box presents and unlimited presents. Mystery box presents are limited in number. That means that the more you draw, the more likely you are to get good rewards. Unlimited presents, however, always have the same drop rate no matter how many you draw.
  • When you exchange Laurel Bouquet, you may get either mystery box presents or unlimited ones.
  • However, when you exchange Rose Bouquet, you'll always get mystery box presents.
  • Liza Escapes, and Liza at the Ball are not included in the box at first. They are added after you refill it.
  • Titania only appears when you trade Rose Bouquet.
  • you can receive up to 21 cakes every 24 hours, starting from 6 AM (ET)
Refilling the Box
Fill the conditions written for each box to refill it with the contents of the next box until you reach Box 5. After that, you can continue to refill Box 5.
