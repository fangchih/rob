How to Obtain Gems[edit]

There are currently 3 methods of obtaining gems.

The first method is from participating in Battle. You can get different amount of gems for reaching a certain battle ranking. You must be minimum level 10 and score at least 500 honor points to make the Battle Rankings and receive Battle Awards. See Battle Rankings for more information.

The second method is from participating in Holy Wars. In recent addition, Holy Wars have been rewarding gems to members in Orders that participate in Holy Wars. You can read more and see previous rewards that have been given out from here.

The third method is from special rewards in certain brigade raid boss events.

Exchange Prizes[edit]

Collect Gems and exchange them for items or Rare cards! Costs for acquiring cards/items are listed next to the reward.
